Geologic Summary

Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation’s Fladeland #31-12TFH is a horizontal well targeting the Three Forks formation, to be drilled in a south southeast direction at a vertical section azimuth of 174.09 degrees for approximme 9,336 feet vertical section. The well is located in the NW ‘/4, NE ‘/4, Section 12 Township 153N Range 92W of Mountrail County, northwest of New Town, North Dakota.
This well spud in on December 6TH, 2014 at 4:00 AM CST. Well site geological services began at 8,500 feet measured depth on December 12TH, 2014 shortly before 1:30 AM CST. Ten,

A thirteen and one half inch wellbore was drilled to 2,300 feet measured depth, and nine and ﬁve eighths inch casing was run and cemented in place. Began logging in the Charles Formation
Logging services began at 8,500 feet measured depth in the Charles formation shortly after 1:30 AM CST on December 12”, 2014. Charles consisted primarily of anhydrite, limestone, and interlaminated dolomitic lenses, along with trace to moderate reddish orange shale. Drill rates ranged from one to two minutes per foot. Background gas averaged 95 units. Base of the last salt was picked at 8,595 feet measured depth, 8,594 feet true vertical depth, with a drill break of .4 to 1 minutes per foot. Samples following consisted primarily of limestone, anhydrite, and minor dolomite, with a varying presence of shale through the Mission Canyon.

Mission Canyon

Mission Canyon was picked at 8,760 feet measured depth, 8,759 feet true vertical depth. The Mission Canyon consisted of predominantly light to medium cream gray, microcrystalline, fairly argillaceous limestone, off white to cream white anhydrite and light tan, micro to cryptocrystalline dolomite. Drill rates averaged l to 1.3 minutes per foot through the Mission Canyon, with 100 units of average background gas. Even white mineral ﬂuorescence and slow,
pinpoint streaming cut present in majority of samples caught.

Lodgepole: 9,409’ MD, 9,408’ TVD, -7,053’ SS
Lodgepole was picked at 9,409 feet measured depth, 9,408 feet true vertical depth using MWD gamma data. Drill rates averaged two minutes per foot in the initial portion of the Lodgepole. Background gas averaged 120 units through the Lodgepole.
