Enerplus Tipi 148-92-22A—21H Exploratory Well —- Mud and Cementing Plan

2. Proposed Casing and Cementing Programs
Casing Program
The surface casing shall consist of new pipe that has been previously tested to 1,000 psi. The surface casing shall be set and cemented at a point not less than 50 feet (15.24 meters) below the base of the Fox Hills Formation. Production or intermediate casing strings shall consist of new pipe that has been previously tested to 2,000 psi. Production liner top would be planned for 100 feet above the KOP. It would be set using a hydraulically-activated liner hanger/packer assembly. After the liner hanger is set and the hanger packer is energized, they would be pressure-tested to 3,000 psi for 30 minutes prior to removal of the setting tool. A
frac string will be set.
