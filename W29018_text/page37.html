November 1“, 2014 at 4:20 pm CT Sidetrack #1 of the Mrachek 21-26-4H was completed in under 16 days of active drilling despite a strike with the upper Bakken shale. Geology of the Mrachek 21-26—4H was consistent with adjacent laterals drilled in the recent weeks. The top of the zone is comprised of a clean, tan to translucent dolomite that becomes more sediment laden lower within the column. The darker color of the lower portion is attributed to the increasing bioturbated, detrital sediment within. Typical of the other laterals in this series, the top half of zone returned the stronger shows of gas and petroleum whenever encountered.
