Existing Lines
At the terminus of the metering and associated piping at each well’s production facilities, the piping will connect to a 6.625” outside diameter (OD) gathering line. This 6.625” OD gathering line traverses approximately 1.5 miles and then connects to an 8.625” OD gathering line. This 8.625” OD gathering extends approximately 2.75 miles and then connects to a 16” OD gathering line. The gas then travels approximately 30 miles before being compressed at the Lodgepole compressor station. This will be the primary ﬂow path of gas delivered to ONEOK from this pad. As noted under “Gathering System”, above, the gas will have secondary ﬂow paths due to the dynamics of ONEOK’s gathering system.
