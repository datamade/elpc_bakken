AFFIDAVIT

STATE OF COLORADO ) COUNTY OF DENVER 355' Steve Stacy, being first duly sworn upon my oath, deposes and states as follows:
That I am Land Manager — Rocky Mountain Division for Samson Resources Company (“Samson”), 370 17th Street, Suite 3000, Denver, Colorado 80202, the applicant for the above Affidavit. Please be advised that Samson Resources is requesting approval of the APDs for the Tanager 17-20-163-95H and the Nuthatch 8-5-163-95H wells in Divide County, North Dakota, which were submitted to the NDIC on November 21, 2011.
Samson owns the drillsite mineral lease which also comprises lands located in the
“off unit" Tanager spacing unit. Please see the attached three leases which will be referred
herein as “Common Leases”. By virtue of the Common Leases, Samson has the right to

develop these leases which cover both the Tanager and Nuthatch units.
Samson has been in good faith negotiations with the surface owner and has been unsuccessful in obtaining a mutually acceptable agreement. Samson has complied with the terms and conditions of North Dakota’s Surface Damage Compensation Act, codified as Chapter 38.11.1 of the North Dakota Century Code. Based on this information, Samson believes that both APDs should be approved.
Samson has plans to build a 6 acre location for the dual pad location site using a
closed loop system with only a dry cuttings pit.
