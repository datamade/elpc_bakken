Conclusion
The Wiley 24-30H well was drilled within the Middle Bakken formation. One hundred foot (100') samples were taken across the entire lateral. The vertical and curve were drilled without any remarkable events other than a Lower Bakken Shale strike at MD 15,882’. Formation samples throughout the entire well were of excellent quality exhibiting good porosity and oil shows in the Middle Bakken. Total gas across the lateral averaged 1987 units producing a constant 5—10’ ﬂare beyond MD 14,800’. A light to medium green oil sheen was observed during drilling operations and over 1500 barrels of oil were captured and stored on location.

This well is currently awaiting completion.
