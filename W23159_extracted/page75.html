The gas volume will be calculated as follows:

3. The measured monthly pad volume will represent the sum ofthe measured monthly well volumes from each well.
4. The custody transfer Qad volume will represent the actual volume delivered through a single pipeline sales or flare meter.
5. The monthly well sales volume will be calculated by multiplying the custody transfer pad volume
times the measured monthly well volume divided by the monthly measured pad volume. 6. Well revenue will be calculated based on the monthly well delivered volume.
