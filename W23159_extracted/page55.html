Lower Bakken Shale

The top of the Lower Bakken Shale was picked at 10,862’ MD, 10,716’ TVD picked 3 feet lower than the prognosis, based on sample changes, ROP, and gamma ray. Background gas ranged from 28-320 units with average showing 119 units. Drilling rates in the Lower Bakken Shale ranged from 018—322 minutes per foot with an average of 0.67 minutes per foot. Gamma ranged from 84-255 API with an average of 129 API. Typical samples were: SHALE: Black, dark brown, smooth, blocky, well compacted, platy in pan, even dull yellow ﬂuorescence, slow broad streaming cut.
