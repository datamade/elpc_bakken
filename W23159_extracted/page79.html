Conclusion

The State 154-102-25-36-9TFH Lateral reached a total depth of 20,328’ MD on January 19, 2015 at 11:00 AM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20281 /INC 87.00 / AZ 180.50 / TVD 10770.21 /VS 9687.10. The Gamma ranged from 31 - 136 API with average of 83 API. Gas averaged 61 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Three Forks formation with poor to fair intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, fast to slow weak milky cuts, and with even dull to bright yellow green ﬂuorescence. The indications of milky cuts and yellow green ﬂuorescents in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a fair oil producer. The State 154-102-25-36-9TFH will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.
