Conclusion

The Avalanche 36—14 MBZ well was drilled within the Middle Bakken (M32) formation. One hundred foot (100') samples were taken across the entire lateral. The vertical and curve were drilled without any remarkable events other than signiﬁcant total gas readings in the 6000 unit range with a single spike in the 7000 unit range in the Lodgepole and persisted to the Upper Bakken Shale. Formation samples throughout the entire well were of excellent quality exhibiting fair to good porosity and oil shows in the Middle Bakken. Total gas across the lateral averaged 293 units. Maximum trip gas at the beginning of the lateral was 6583 units.
This well is currently awaiting completion.
