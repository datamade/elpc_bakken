2/11/09

DRILLING PROCEDURE TEXAKOTA, INC. #4—5 H. BORSTAD
Miru
Drive 60’ of 20” conductor pipe
Drill w/14 %” PDC bit & gelled fresh water to 2,000’. Set 10 3A” 40.5 # casing & cement to surface w/ﬁall returns.
Recalibrate Pathﬁnder for new location G.L. and KB.
Drill out w/9 7/8” PDC bit & oil invert emulsion mud to top of KOP/Radius at 9215’ MD. POOH & RIH w/multishot — recalibrate Pathﬁnder.
PU BHA #1 w/Pathﬁnder & drill 12.5 degree radius, w/9 7/8” tricone bit(s) to a MD of 10,435’ & TVD of 9683’ within the Bakken Sand member. TD will be 500’ horizontally into the sand FROM the entry point at top of the Bakken Sand.
Entry point at top of the Bakken Sand will be at 9,935’ MD & TVD of 9,674’.
Set 29# & 32#, 7 5/ 8”casing. Set heavier casing across the salt sections. Cement in three stages. Cement 1St stage from TD to below the Charles Salt. Cement the 2nd stage from base of Charles Salt to above the Charles Salt & Pine Salt. Cement the 3rd stage from above Pine Salt to above both the Dakota Sand & above the Mowry Shale.

Lay down 4 1/2” DP & PU 3 V2” DP, w/BHA #2, including Pathﬁnder PZIG tool and 6 1/8” or 6 1/1” PDC bit.

Drill approximately 3,188’ of horizontal leg w/9.6—10.2# NACL brine. Use gel sweeps as necessary to clean leg. Set 4 1/2” ﬂush joint casing and ﬁve - Baker Hughes isolation swell packers/ports at approximately 638’ intervals.
Prior to drilling #4-5 H. Borstad, obtain an open-hole log waiver from NDIC. Log hole from radius to surface w/GR—Neutron & Cement Bond log.
RDMO drilling rig. Frac well, ﬂow back and clean-up leg.
MIRU workover rig. RIH with tubing, set pumpjack.
