Syverson 150-99-36-25-10H SWSW S36 T150N R99W McKenzie County, ND

To whom it may concern:
This notice is intended to conﬁrm that the proposed completion consisting of hydraulic fracture treatment on the above mentioned well is in compliance with EPA UlC Program Guidance #84 dated February 2014 regarding oil and gas hydraulic fracturing activities using diesel fuels. Newfield is aware of the EPA guidance and does not intend to inject “diesel fuels" in any quantity. Newﬁeld has conﬁrmed with its fracturing service provider that diesel fuels are not included in the chemicals injected during fracturing treatment of Newﬁeld operated wells. Diesel fuels are deﬁned below by Chemical Abstract Service
Registry Number:

1) 68334-30-5 Primary Name: Fuels, diesel
2) 68476-34-6 Primary Name: Fuels, diesel, No. 2 3) 68476-30-2 Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2
4) 68476-31-3 Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4
5) 8008-20-6 Primary Name: Kerosene

In anticipation of EPA's diesel fuel guidance that was in development for several years, Newfield had already prohibited the use of diesel fuels in our hydraulic fracturing ﬂuids systems. This self-imposed prohibition was clariﬁed with formal notices to our respective pressure pumping service providers. Newﬁeld discloses all of our chemicals pumped to the Ground Water Protection Council's national registry FracFocus and voluntarily submitted 25 wells in North Dakota before the April 1, 2012 effective date of North Dakota Administrative Code 43-02-03-27.1.g.
