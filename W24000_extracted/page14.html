STATE OF Colorado )
) § COUNTY OF Denver )
David Campbell, of lawful age, being duly sworn, on his oath says that he is Denver’s Division Completions Manager at EOG Resources, Inc, (E06) 600 17‘h Street, Suite 1000N, Denver, Colorado 80202 and duly authorized to make this Affidavit in its behalf.
WHEREAS, EOG has applied to the NDlC for permits to drill the Hardscrabble 16-0409H well.
WHEREAS, the United States Environmental Protection Agency identifies diesel fuel as any compound identified with the following Chemical Abstract Service Registry Numbers (CASRN) 68334-30—5 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel), 68476-34—6 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel, No. 2), 68476-30-2 (Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2), 68476-31-3 (Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4), and, 8008—20-6 (Primary Name: Kerosene); and,
WHEREAS, EOG utilizes hydraulic fracturing techniques to complete drilled oil and gas wells.
NOW THEREFORE, I hereby affirm that EOG does not utilize diesel fuel, as defined, in
fluids used for underground hydraulic fracturing; no diesel fuel will be used in hydraulic fracturing of the subject wells.
