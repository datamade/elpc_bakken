AFFIDAVIT

Effective 1 January 2014, XTO Energy lnc., Western Division, Bakken Operations, hereby attests that for any hydraulic fracturing operation; we will not use diesel fuel in the form of any compound identified with the following Chemical Abstract Service Registry numbers:

68334-30-5 (Primary Name: Fuels. diesel) 68476—34-6 (Primary Name: F uels, diesel, No. 2) 68476—30-2 (Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2) 68476-31-3 (Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4)
8008304) (Primary Name: Kerosene)

in addition, XTO Energy, Inc. understands that we must obtain an Underground injection Control, Class ll injection permit from the North Dakota industrial Commission prior to commencing any hydraulic fracturing stimulation in which any amount of the above are injected.
