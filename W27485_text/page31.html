General:
1. Monthly downtime will be monitored for all commingled wells to determine the total number of producing hours for each well.

LACT meter used will be proved according to the section below entitled "Current Emerald Oil Sales LACT Proving".
Well oil allocation meters will be proved on a quarterly basis using either a positive displacement proving loop or to a test tank.
Volumes will be reported to appropriate agencies through routine oil production reports filed monthly.

Current Emerald Oil Sales LACT Proving:
The time between LACT proving depends on the amount of oil sold through the pipeline/truck LACT
as follows:
1. LACT ships 2,000 bbls or more in a month: LACT proved every month.
2. LACT ships less than 2,000 bbls in a month: LACT is proved every quarter.
3. in addition, to establish a comparative meter factor for a new LACT, the initial proving is followed by a second proving a month later, regardless of how much oil is shipped through the LACT for that month.

Gas Continuous Metering: Individual Well “Monthly Well Gas Production": 1. Volumes of gas will be metered at a dedicated treater (at central facility) using an orifice meter. 2. Volumes of gas production will be the volume continuously metered for each well to determine gas production.
