Coming out of the shoe, the initial formation dip was projected at ~90.3 degrees and ~17 5.25 degrees azimuth. From the shoe to 12,224’ MD it was determined the dip was 90.0 degrees aﬁer tracking the Cool to Ratty gamma markers. At 11:00 AM MST on September 26, 2014 the decision was made to stop drilling due to well bore crossing the state designated Hard line.
