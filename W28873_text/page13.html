Hess is currently in the conceptual phase of evaluation of fleet vehicle bi-fuel conversions. The company is looking at the possibility of using compressed natural gas from TGP to fuel vehicles. Currently, one refueling location has been scoped.
Because there is only one refueling location for compressed natural gas, it would be difficult to make this project economically viable. Cost savings come from using compressed gas, rather than diesel fuel or gasoline.
Individual production facilities are not being considered for use as compressed natural gas stations at this time.
Hess will continue to evaluate this technology.
