Background

The wells are located on the same drilling pad but have diverse ownership, separate ﬁxed royalty rate and revenue distribution. This facility will be constructed so that the each well with have its own treater with individual metering for oil and water production, which will then be comingled into a single tank battery from which the oil will be sold. Recycled oil will go to a “junk treater” for further separation/treatment. The junk treater will have no metering equipment. Gas production will be sold from each well separately. Comingling approval will allow for installation of only one LACT unit for this site.
