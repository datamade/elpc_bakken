Texakota, lnc. (Texakota) ﬁled with the North Dakota Industrial Commission — Oil and Gas Division (Commission) on February 21, 2008 an application for a Stripper Well Property Determination for the above captioned well.
Information contained in the application indicates that the above mentioned well is a property pursuant to statute and rule, and Texakota has elected to designate said well as a separate property for stripper well purposes. The well produced from a well depth greater than 10000 fect. During the qualifying period, February 1, 2007 through January 1, 2008 the well produced at a maximum efﬁcient rate and the average daily production from the well was 15.0 barrels of oil per day during this period.

It is therefore determined that the above captioned well qualiﬁes as a “Stripper Well Property” pursuant to Section 57-5 1.1-01 of the North Dakota Century Code. This determination is
applicable only to the Bakken Pool in and under said property.
