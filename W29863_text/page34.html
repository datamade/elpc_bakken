EVALUATION PROGRAM
Logs: The logging program will be run by Schlumberger and will consist of their Platform Express plus BHC Sonic from 11342' to 2160'. The Gamma Ray and resistivity log will be pulled back to surface.

The evaluation program may change at the discretion of the well site geologist.

COMPLETION

The planned top of the 4.5” 11.6” production liner will be 50-100 it above the kick off point. This liner will be cemented throughout the lateral and a wet shoe sub will be set at the toe of the liner. The frac string will be 12.75# P-110 with TSHP threads and stung into a PBR run with the
liner hanger prior to the fracturing treatment. Current plans are for 28 stages using a plug and perf approach.

ABNORMAL CONDITIONS

No abnormal temperatures or pressures are anticipated. Hydrogen sulﬁde (HZS) may be encountered in the Mission Canyon Formation at a TVD of approximately 9620‘. Safety equipment will be in operation at approximately 9120'.
Maximum anticipated bottom hole pressure equals approximately 5834 psi (calculated at 0.53 psi/foot at Middle Bakken target depth, 11008’) and maximum anticipated surface pressure equals approximately 3412 psi (bottom hole pressure minus the pressure of a partially evacuated hole calculated at 0.22 psi/foot at Middle Bakken target depth). The intermediate design casing will be run and cemented before drilling the target lateral.
Reservoir temperature is anticipated to be 242°F.
