Conclusion

The Simpson 151-102-5—8-10TFH Lateral reached a total depth of 20,786’ MD on December 3, 2014 at 10:45 AM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20786 / INC 90.00 / AZ 179.50 I TVD 10910.66 / VS 10125.44. The Gamma ranged from 32 and 141 API and averaged 89 API. Gas averaged 62 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Three Forks formation with poor to fair intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, light to fair brown oil staining in samples, and weak to fair milky cuts and even dull to bright yellow green ﬂuorescence. The indications of good gases, a ﬂare l-lO feet observed while drilling, and fair oil shows in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a good oil producer. The Simpson 15 1-102-5-8-10TF H will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.
