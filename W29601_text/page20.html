ONEOK’s low pressure gathering system is near capacity in this area due to several new wells recently connected. It is projected that the system will have capacity when this well is ready for production due to decline on the existing wells gathered on this segment of the system and ONEOK’s project listed in the following paragraph.
ONEOK is in the process of adding compressors at its Lost Bridge compressor station in Section 14, T147N, R96W, Dunn County and installing a 16” OD low pressure gathering line that will start in T145N, R94W, Dunn County (Dunn Center) and end at the inlet of the Lost Bridge compressor station. Construction of these facilities will redirect gas that currently ﬂows on the secondary ﬂow paths of the K Stroh 1 Pad to flow on the new facilities. Although this does not directly increase the capacity of the secondary flow paths of the K Stroh 1 Pad, it redirects gas that currently ﬂows on the same pipelines to a different compressor station, in essence reducing volumes transported on the ﬂow paths for these wells. ONEOK is also planning on constructing a new discharge line from its Lost Bridge compressor station that will deliver some of the gas currently processed at ONEOK’S Grasslands Processing Plant to ONEOK’s Garden Creek Processing P1ant(s).
