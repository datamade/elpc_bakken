EMERALD
OIL
Commingled Production Allocation Method for Continuous Measurement
Emerald Oil will use the following procedures for allocating production for the associated continuous
measurement wells commingled at the Talon/Slugger Mid Production Pad:
General:
1.
Monthly downtime will be monitored for all commingled wells to determine the total number of producing hours for each well.
Oil Continuous Measurement: Individual Well "Allocated Monthly Well OIL SALES":
1. 2.
Volumes of oil will be metered at a dedicated treater (at central facility) using coriolis meters. The total oil volume leaving the central production facility will be metered by LACT unit prior to trucking oil or entry into an oil gathering pipeline system.
a. LACT metered oil volumes for the month are termed "Actual Monthly Oil Sales". Determine a theoretical monthly oil production volume for each continuously measured well as follows:
a. Theoretical Monthly Oil Production = Total oil volume continuously measured for a month with Volume corrected as necessary by responsible operations/metering personnel for known upsets/malfunctioning of equipment or measurement devices that impact accuracy of well oil volumes.
Sum all the individual well "Theoretical Monthly Oil Production" volumes to determine the "Total Theoretical Monthly Oil Production" for the central production facility.
Calculate a sales factor by dividing the total "Actual Monthly Oil Sales" by the "Total Theoretical Monthly Oil Production" volumes for the central production facility corrected as necessary for any time well was producing but measurement was not operational.
Determine "Allocated Monthly Oil Production" for each well by multiplying the respective
individual well "Theoretical Monthly Oil Production" volume by the sales factor calculated in step (5) above.
LACT meter used in the measurement will be proved according to the section below entitled "Current Emerald Oil Sales LACT Proving".
Well oil allocation meters will be proved on a quarterly basis using either a positive displacement proving loop or to a test tank.
Volumes will be reported to appropriate agencies through routine oil production reports filed monthly.
Current Emerald Oil Sales LACT Proving: The time between LACT proving depends on the amount of oil sold through the pipeline/truck LACT
as follows:
1. LACT ships 2,000 bbls or more in a month: LACT proved every month.
2. LACT ships less than 2,000 bbls in a month: LACT is proved every quarter.
3. In addition, to establish a comparative meter factor for a new LACT, the initial proving is
followed by a second proving a month later, regardless of how much oil is shipped through the LACT for that month.

Gas Continuous Measurement: Individual Well "Monthly Well Gas Production": 1. Volumes of gas will be metered at a dedicated treater (at central facility) using an orifice meter. 2. Volumes of gas production will be the volume continuously metered for each well to determine gas production.
