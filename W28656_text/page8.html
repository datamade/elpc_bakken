Conclusion
Overall the well had poor to fair gas and oil shows. In the Three Forks formation the average ROP was around 0.5 min/ﬂ. Lateral #1 ﬁnished with 79.03% of the wellbore being in the Three Forks target. The wellbore was estimated to be around 14.33% being in the out the top of the Three Forks target window. The wellbore was estimated to be around 6.64% being in the Internal 1 below the Three Forks target window. We are very excited to see the results once the well is put into production.
