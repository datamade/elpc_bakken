6/10/09

DRILLING PROCEDURE TEXAKOTA, INC. #3 H. BORSTAD

Miru
Drive 60’ of 20” conductor pipe
Drill w/14 3%.” PDC bit & gelled fresh water to 2,000’. Set 10 3A” 40.5 # casing & cement to surface w/ﬁJll returns.
Recalibrate Pathﬁnder for new location G.L. and KB.
Drill out w/9 7/8” PDC bit & oil invert emulsion mud to top of KOP/Radius at 9,298.6’ MD. POOH & RIH w/multishot * recalibrate Pathﬁnder.
PU BHA #1 w/Pathﬁnder & drill 12.5 degree radius, w/9 7/8” tricone bit(s) to a MD of 10,510.3’ & TVD of 9,762.0’ within the Lower Bakken/Upper Three Forks. TD will be
590’ horizontally into the target zone FROM the entry point of the Lower Bakken/Upper Three Forks target.

Entry point in Lower Bakken/Upper Three Forks target will be at approximately 9,909.0’ MD & 9,745.0’ TVD.

Set 29# & 32#, 7 5/8”casing. Set heavier casing across the salt sections. Cement in three stages. Cement 1st stage from TD to below the Charles Salt. Cement the 2nd stage from base of Charles Salt to above the Charles Salt & Pine Salt. Cement the 3rd stage from above Pine Salt to above both the Dakota Sand & above the Mowry Shale.
Lay down 4 '/2” DP & PU 3 V2” DP, w/BHA #2, including Pathﬁnder PZIG tool and 6 1/8” or 6 W’ PDC bit.
Drill approximately 3,400’ of horizontal leg w/9.6# -10.2# NACL brine. Use gel sweeps as necessary to clean leg. Set 4 V2” ﬂush joint casing and six to seven — Baker Hughes isolation swell packers/ports, at approximately 485’— 566’ intervals, depending on number of packers/ports used to complete leg.
Prior to drilling Texakota, Inc. #3 H. Borstad, obtain an open-hole log waiver from the NDIC. Log the hole from radius to surface, w/GR-Neutron & Cement Bond log.
RDMO drilling rig. Frac well, ﬂow back and clean-up leg.
MIRU workover rig. RIH with tubing. Set pumpj ack. Place on production.
