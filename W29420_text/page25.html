Lodgepole

The Lodgepole Formation top was observed at 9,579’ MD (9,578’ TVD; -7,659’ SS), 4’ lower than the prognosis. It was identiﬁed by GR signature, differential pressure, torque, ROP, and cuttings lithology. ROP through the Lodgepole Formation averaged 0.7 minutes per foot (86 feet/hour). Background gas readings averaged 38 units with a peak recorded reading of 59 units. No unexpected hydrocarbon shows were observed in the Lodgepole Formation.
