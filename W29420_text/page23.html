GEOLOGICAL SUMMARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation’s State 31-3-1H is a single lateral horizontal oil well targeting the Middle Bakken and is located in NWNE Section 3, T1 SON, R104W, in McKenzie County, North Dakota. It was spudded on 02 Nov 2014. Total depth of 21,775' MD; 10,369.24' TVD; 10,023.48' VS reached at 1837 hrs MST 19
Nov 2014.

Meredith Remmich, Alan Chappell, Scott Strahm, Timothy Engelking, and Stephanie Davis of Neset Consulting Service, Inc. provided geological services ﬁ’om a measure depth of 7,450’. One set of samples was collected in thirty foot intervals for the vertical and curve, and in one hundred foot intervals during the lateral. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Survey core library in Grand Forks, North Dakota. Pason Gas Detection was utilized for gas detection.
