Conclusion
Overall the well had good to excellent gas shows and fair to good oil shows. Shows were consistent regardless of location in target zone. Sample cuts through the lateral were good to excellent as well. The overall wellbore was almost entirely within the target zone with the exception of a few entries into the Internal 1. There appeared to be periods of fracturing while drilling this well which is a good sign for production. Porosity and permeability looked very consistent as well and fracking operations should have no problem opening up the target zone. Based on what was seen while drilling, with the possible fractures, good drilling shows, and excellent trip shows, it is my opinion this well shows very good promise of being a good
producer.
