Three Forks 9,506’ MD 9,366 ’ T VD -7,041 ’ SS
The Three Forks was picked from gamma change. Samples were dolomite, gray, brown, light brown, cream, tan, very sandy, argillaceous at times, slightly calcareous, predominately microsucrosic, earthy at times, friable to ﬁrm, scattered dark brown oil staining, poor intercrystalline porosity, scattered to abundant pale yellow green ﬂuorescence, with weak to strong ﬂuorescing cuts. Gas ranged from 191 to 935 units with connection/downtime gases of 471 to 2008 units. The Three Forks came in 10’ high to prognosis. The curve was drilled to 9,674’ MD on September 5, 2014. Casing was set at 9,657’ MD, 9,385.19’ TVD and 456.66’ VS. BHL ofcasing was 816.24’ FNL & 1,303.45’ FWL in Sec 13, T159N, and R96W.
