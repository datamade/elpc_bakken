Oasis requests permission for suspension of drilling for up to 90 days for the referenced Well under NDAC 43-02-03-55. The casing will be set at a depth pro-approved by the NDIC per the Application for Permit to Drill NDAC 43-02-03-21. No saltwater will be used In the drilling and cementing operations of the surface casing.
