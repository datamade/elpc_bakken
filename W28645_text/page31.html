LATERAL
The drilling of the lateral began on Sunday, October 12, 2014. The wellbore would begin rising gradually in the formation following the drilling of the shoe. A downslide was put in at 10,820’ MD to counter this rise. This downslide would send the wellbore dropping in the formation. A high side slide was put in at 11,038’ MD to level the wellbore off in the formation. This slide would send the wellbore rising in the formation. 3 downslides were put in at 11,712’ MD to send the wellbore back down in
the formation.

This drop would continue and send the wellbore back down towards the target window. An adjustment was made to the target window based on the gamma marker seen at 11,820’ MD to 89.26 degrees. A high side slide was put in at 12,632’ MD to level the wellbore off in the formation. This slide would send the wellbore rising gradually in the formation. 2 downslides were put in at 12,904’ MD to counter the rise seen in the wellbore.

These slides would send the wellbore dropping in the formation until a deﬂection up was taken at 13,404’ MD. These slides would send the wellbore rising in the formation. 2 downslides were put in at 13,566’ Ivﬂ) and 13,697’ Nﬂ) to level the wellbore off. These slides would send the wellbore dropping in the formation until a high side slide was put in at 14,516’ MD. This slide would send the wellbore rising in the formation.
A ﬁirther adjustment to the Middle Bakken Target Window was made to 89.26 degrees at this point. 3 downslides were put in at 14,705’ MD following this adjustment in an attempt to send the wellbore back into the target window. The wellbore would continue dropping in the formation. 3 additional slides were put in at 15,17 8’ MD.

A high side slide was put in at 16,313’ MD to level the wellbore off in the

formation. This slide would send the wellbore rising gradually in the formation. A further adjustment was made to the Middle Bakken Target Window at this point due to unexpected gamma markers.

Two downslides were put in at 16,691 ’ MD to send the wellbore dropping in the formation. This drop would continue and bring the wellbore just above the target windows The wellbore would begin rising following a high side slide at 18,298’ MD A downslide was put in to counter this rise at 18,582’ MD. The wellbore would re-enter the target window at 19,555’ MD. A high side slide was put in at 18,897 ’ MD to level the wellbore off in the target window. The remainder of the well was drilled in rotation with the wellbore dropping in rotation following a deﬂection down at 19,264’ MD.
