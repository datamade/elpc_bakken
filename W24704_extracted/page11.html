Flaring Alternatives Considered
In the event that a pipeline connection is not going to be possible, Slawson has evaluated a method for liquid natural gas extraction for ﬂared gas. This method relies on compression and refrigeration to remove C3+ products from the ﬂared gas stream. Based on the sites gas composition it is possible to reduce VOC emissions on site by nearly 55%, and reduce CO; by 20% using these methods, and reclaim the majority of the NGL components of the gas stream. Any remaining unprocessed gas volumes will be sent to 98% combustion efﬁcient ﬂare.
