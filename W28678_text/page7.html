Conclusion
Overall the well had fair to good gas and oil shows with periods of poor shows. Shows were very dependent on the wellbore’s location in zone with the best shows appearing to come from right at the base of the clean gamma. Sample cuts through the lateral were fair to good and correlated closely with oil and gas shows. The overall wellbore was completely within the target zone with the exception of approximately 600’ of the second sidetrack going below target. The vast majority is very close to the base of the clean gamma which had the best show throughout the drilling of this well. Porosity and permeability looked very consistent through the well and fracking operations should have no problem opening up the streak showing the best gas and oil. Based on what was seen while drilling and the excellent trip shows, it is my opinion this well shows very good promise in being a good producer.

The Bjella 4-13H is currently awaiting completion.
