Geologic Summary

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation, Stenehjem 41—3HU well was drilled in the Bully Field with primary interest in the Middle Bakken Formation. The well was drilled by Pioneer 74 ﬂex rig. The well is located in the NENE quarter of section 3 T148N R99W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. The Stenehjem 41~3HU was spudded on January 30, 2015 at 7:00 AM CST. The well reached a total depth of 20,863’ MD on February 12, 2015. Geological services began at 9,450’ MD in the Charles Formation on February 3, 2015 and continued to TD. Samples were caught by mudloggers throughout the well. All samples were washed, cataloged, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Society in Grand Forks, North Dakota. Total Gas LTD gas equipment was used to monitor gas throughout the well in correspondence with NOV RigSense EDR.
