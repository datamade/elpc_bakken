Gas Plant - OneOK’s System
OneOK’s system is a network of low pressure and high pressure lines connecting gathered wells to a gas processing facility. Field gas is gathered and transported through the system to OneOK’s Stateline plant (Sec. 21—155N-103W) for processing. The system has interconnections to allow offloading excess gas
volumes to Hess’ Tioga plant (Sec. 26-157N—95W) and to Hiland Partners’ Watford City Plant (Sec. 6151N-102W).
