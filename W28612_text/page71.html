Bartleson 11-18H CTB Commingling Procedure

Testing one well at a time through test treater. Oil is a 72 hour test into a speciﬁed tank, and oil is trucked. Produced water is a 72 hour test into a speciﬁed test, and produced water is trucked. Gas is a 72 hour test and is measured from an allocation meter on the sales line and the ﬂare line
to acquire total gas ﬂow from test treater. Allocation gas meters will be proved quarterly by Whiting Oil and Gas.
Well tests will be done continuously for the ﬁrst 15 days, then monthly thereafter.
