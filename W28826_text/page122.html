RUNNING PROCEDURE FOR CEMENTED LINERS
0 Drill well to TD; circ bottoms up. Make wiper trip to 7” intermediate csg shoe. POH & spot kill mud in vertical hole. POH & LD drill pipe & BHA.
o PU 4-1/2” liner. PU setting tool & RIH on 4” drill pipe
0 Land liner on bottom; PU & set hanger. Circ bottoms up.
0 RU cmtg company. Pump cmt slurry per design (details listed below).
0 Land liner wiper plug and latch into landing collar with 500 to 1000 psi over circulating pressure
0 Slowly increase pressure and rupture burst disk in liner wiper plug
0 Pump an additional 3 to 5 bbls to achieve wet shoe, shut down and check floats
0 Hold 500psi on floats while stinging out from TOL, at first sign of pressure drop, stop and pump 30 bbls of clean field brine, continue to sting out from TOL and circ cmt out 4” XT by 7” annulus.
o Press test liner top packer. POH & LD setting tool. PU polish mill & RIH
0 Tag TOL. Dress off tie back receptacle. Circ bottoms up. POH & LD 4” DP.
0 PU & RIH w/ 4-1/2" frac string. Circ hole clean at TOL. Space out frac string. Circulate packer fluid on backside. Land tie back string. ND BOPE; NU WH.
o Cementing details listed below using a 7,181’ lateral.

PLUG AND PERF COMPLETION PROCEDURE IN CEMENTED LINER

RU high pressure pump truck. Pressure test 7”x 4-1/2” annulus to 3000 psi for 30 mins. Pressure test 4-1/2” frac string to 9500 psi for 30 mins (holding 500 psi on backside)
RU pressure pumping services/ frac crew. RU wireline truck w/ mast pole.
Pump 10K composite bridge plug & perf guns (3 sets 2’ X 6 spf) to TD. Set plug & position guns. Fire perf guns & POH.
Drop ball and pump ﬁrst frac stage
Repeat WL run w/ plug and perf guns. Estimate 30-40 total frac stages per cemented liner.
