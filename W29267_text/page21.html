Notwithstanding any provisions to the contrary contained in the Surface Damage and Release Agreement, the following provisions shall control and take precedence:
More speciﬁcally the Right-of-Way for the road access and the well pad location will be located as speciﬁed and illustrated on the attached Exhibit “A”. Operator and Owner hereby agree that nothing contained in this Surface Damage Settlement and Release Agreement shall be
deemed as giving the Operator the right to place any pipelines on the premises. Any pipelines on the premises shall be by separate negotiated agreement between Owner and Operator.
Upon receipt of written notice of ﬁnal abandonment of all wells, Owner shall have the right to choose whether to leave the well site and/or access road or have it restored to as near as practical to the original ground contour existing as of the date of this agreement. Owner shall have 10 (ten) business days to respond in writing to Operator as to any decision to retain the well site and/or access road in its current condition. In the event that Owner elects not to retain the well site and/or access road, all disturbed surfaces will be re-seeded by Operator as many times as is required to re-establish vegetation to its original cover, nature, density and species of plants. Speciﬁcally, all disturbed surfaces will be re-seeded as per the Owner’s speciﬁcations. Operator shall remove all its machinery, above-ground appurtenances and structures within 12 months of
ﬁnal abandonment of all wells.
Operator agrees that it, in all operations conducted by it, or its successors, upon the Property, it will protect ground water sources and surface soils from contamination. Operator shall make reasonable efforts to save all topsoil and replace any which may be lost during drilling
operations, whether the wells are completed as aproducer or as a dry hole.
Operator shall not use any fresh water, including but not limited to, groundwater or water ﬁ-om springs, dams or wells, from the premises or water from fresh water wells drilled or to be drilled on the Owner’s premises without the written consent of the Owner. If written permission is given, and a fresh water well is drilled on the Owner’s premises, the well, along with the associated water well casing pipe in the well, becomes the property of the Owner upon completion of the drilling and completion operations, at the Owner’s option. At the conclusion of the Operators full development of the well site, Owner shall have 10 (ten) business days to respond in writing to Operator as to its decision to retain the fresh water well. If operations by the Operator on the leased premises under the provisions of this Agreement cause damage to any water well of the Owner or restrict the supply of water to Owner or its tenants for domestic, livestock or agricultural purpose, or which would do injury to any potable ground watersupply, Operator shall pay for all damages, including the cost of one or more new water wells if deemed necessary.
Operator shall comply with all laws of the state of North Dakota; Operations shall be conducted so they do not change the character of, or cause pollution to ponds, reservoirs, water wells,
streams, springs and other water ways.
Operator, its successors and assigns shall be solely responsible to maintain the access road granted herein. Such maintenance shall include but not be limited to grading, graveling, snow removal, culvert and cattle guard repairs. Further, snow removal shall not result in gravel, scoria or road surface material being placed on Owner’s land outside the road easement area. Operator shall properly fence the property described on Exhibit “A”. Operator, its successors and assigns shall be solely responsible to maintain such fences.
Upon request of Owner, Operator shall control by appropriate spraying, clipping or other appropriate means all noxious weeds that grow within the lands described on Exhibit “A”.
Well pad location surface damages will be paid prior to commencement of actual operations of constructing or reconstruction of the well sites. Damages relating to construction of the access road on said Lands will be paid infull priorto beginning road construction or improvements to an existing road.
