DETAILS OF WORK Hess respectfully requests to store surface hole cuttings from spud thru surface casing setting depth on location for later use. Under NDIC 43-02-03-21 this interval will be drilled with freshwater-based drilling mud. Hess will utilize closed loop solids processing equipment in this interval, allowing cuttings to be fairly dry and easily transported from the cuttings processing equipment to a designated spoil bank.
The spoil bank will consist of stockpiles of excess cut/ﬁll dirt from location construction and the fresh water drill cuttings. If additional drying is required. an additive mixture of approved solid material will be incorporated. Prior to any use of these stored cuttings the material will be mixed for uniformity. Three to five separate samples will be taken from the storage area and fluid toxicity bioassay tests will be performed with accepted practices and standards by an independent lab. Sample results will be forwarded to the NDIC and Hess.
Pending approval from the NDIC after lab test reviewal, the spoil bank cuttings mixture may be used either for the 4' cover material over the oil base cuttings pit, to re-contour the disturbed area from permitted activity, or a combination of the two procedures. The spoil bank cuttings mixture will be covered with top soil and planted
per instruction.
