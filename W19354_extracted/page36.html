DRILLING PROGRAM
1. Drill hole as prescribed for surface casing; run and cement surface casing with minimum of 35% excess volume on lead cement and minimum of 15% excess volume on tail cement.
2. Displace hole with oil base mud and drill to KOP.
3. Kick well off, building angle as per enclosed directional plan, to at or near proposed production casing setting depth.
4. Run and cement production casing with volumes required to cover the Dakota formation (see table above and enclosed formation top estimates).
5. Test casing to 1500 psig. Drill cement and float equipment.
6. Drill horizontal section to proposed TD.
7. Run 4-1/2” production liner utilizing swell packers for zone isolation in conjunction with sliding sleeves and/or plug and perf, set liner hanger @ +/- 100’ above KOP
8. Install frac string
9. Rig down and move out drilling rig.
