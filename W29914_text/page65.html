GEOLOGIC SUMNIARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation Curtis Moen 41-26-3H is a development well with a surface location in the NENE of Section 26 T1 SON R100W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. The Curtis Moen is a two-section lateral well which targets the Middle Bakken Formation. The Curtis Moen spudded January 15‘”, 2015 and reached a total depth of 20,664' MD on January 5‘“, 2015. Wellsite geological services began at 9,300 feet in the Base Last Salt at 10:00 AM January 18‘”, 2015. Thirty foot samples were collected in the vertical phase, and ﬁfty foot samples were collected in the lateral.
