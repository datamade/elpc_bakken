Structurally, the Roff Operating Osterberg #2—16 H1 Sidetrack #1 drilled as expected in the Wildcat Field. Well control data gathered from this well has provided solid structural and TVD data needed to refine the Wildcat Field Middle Bakken structure. It should be noted that Sidetrack #1 exhibited a significant diprate increase as drilling progressed North; at some point the Middle Bakken Target structure will roll over and start downdipping, at which point an anticlinal oil trapping structure could be located and exploited.
SUMMARY AND CONCLUSIONS:
1.) The Roff Operating Osterberg #2-16 H1 Sidetrack #1 was a successful wellbore within the Mississippian Middle Bakken Target of the Madison Fm. Entering the Middle Bakken Target at 6350’ MD/5843’ (-4125’) TVD, the Sidetrack #1 wellbore opened a total of 3602’ of 6” openhole in the Middle Bakken Target after Target entry with 100% of the total within the 8' thick Middle Bakken Target drilling Window.
2.) Oil show indicators consisted of moderately slow to fast streaming and diffuse cuts with some accompanying residual ring cuts. and trace light to medium brown oil spotting. Middle Bakken Dolomite intercrystalline porosity ranged between 6-8%. Gas values ranged between 50 and 100u.
