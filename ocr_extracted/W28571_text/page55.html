Drill Procedures
Move in and rig up.
Spud well with l3 1/2” tooth bit and drill to 480’+or—. Circ. 1/2 hr with rig pump.
Make wiper trip.
Drill to casing TD @ 800’ +.
Trip out and lay down 8” DC’s.
Rig up casers and run 9 5/8” casing.
Circ. Casing on bottom ‘/2 hr. with rig pump.
Cement surface casing.
W.O.C. 3 hrs.
Cut off casing and weld on head.
Nipple up BOP.
Test BOP.
Install wear bushing.
Pick up 8 3%” PDC bit, MWD and mud motor set at (1.5). Trip in.
Drill cement, ﬂoat and shoe.
Drill 8 3%” hole to KOP @ 5282’ (Keep vertical hole under 2*). Trip out (Strap out).
Rig up and run logs
