TO: Todd Holweger, Permit Manager North Dakota Industrial Commission
RE: Kings Canyon/Teton/LaCanyon Quad Pad SENE of Section 34-151N-95W

Burlington Resources will perform the required interim reclamation and reseeding of the unused portion of the well pad not needed for production operations to allow for at least 200’ right of way form the centerline of Highway 23 for any future expansion of said Highway. Burlington Resources submitted the proposal request to the DOT for this project. A copy of the letter and preliminary Interim Reclamation Layout is attached which supports the statements above.
