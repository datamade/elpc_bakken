The wellbore continued to drill within the top 2’ of the target window until about 20,980’ at which point it was allowed to rotate out. Drilling rates ranged from 0.23 to 4.77 minutes per foot and averaged 0.47 minutes per foot. Gamma ranged ﬁom 71 to 125 API and averaged 103 API. Total gas averaged 604 units. Samples were generally sandstone: very light tan, cream, very ﬁne grain, subrounded to subangular, moderately sorted, moderately cemented, dolornitic cement, scattered pyrite; 5% light blue green shale, pyritic, 5% siltstone; fair porosity, patchy dull white ﬂuorescence, cloudy green blue cut. As drilling continued the wellbore rose in true vertical depth and the samples had more greenish shale.
Two trips were made throughout the entire lateral One trip was at 18,546’ measured depth due to motor failure. A new bit and motor were picked up. Once back on bottom trip gas spiked to 561 units. Another trip was made at 19,427’ measured depth due to MWD tool failure. A new motor and MWD tool were picked up. Once back on bottom trip gas spiked 3968 units.

The Kaldahl 44-12TFH reached a total measured depth of 21,051 at 5am on

December 30, 2014. The Kaldahl 44-12TFH is currently awaiting completion.
