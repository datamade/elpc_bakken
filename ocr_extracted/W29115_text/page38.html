37.080 Siting of Production Facilities on Fill
If production facilities, such as the tank battery, treater, or flare pit, have to be located on the fill portion of the well pad and the well pad is constructed between winter freeze-up and spring thaw or consists of material that will not meet T99, Method C compaction specifications, the area beneath the facilities will be lined with a 300 PSI liner that is bound below and above with commercial bentonite or natural bentonite clay, as illustrated below.
