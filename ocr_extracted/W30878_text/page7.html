COMPLETION

The planned top of the 4.5” 11.6” production liner will be 50-100 ft above the kick off point. This liner will be cemented throughout the lateral and a wet shoe sub will be set at the toe of the liner. The frac string will be 4.5”, 12.75# P-llO with TSHP threads and stung into a
PBR run with the liner hanger prior to the fracturing treatment. Current plans are for 28 stages using a plug and perf approach.
No diesel fuels, as defined by the United States Environmental Protection Agency (U SEPA), will be used in the hydraulic fracturing of this well.
USEPA identiﬁes diesel fuels as any compound identiﬁed with the following Chemical Abstract Service Registry Numbers:
68334-30-5 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel) 68476-34-6 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel, No. 2) 68476-30-2 (Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2) 68476-31-3 (Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4) 8008-20-6 (Primary Name: Kerosene)
. ABNORMAL CONDITIONS
No abnormal temperatures or pressures are anticipated. Hydrogen sulﬁde (H2S) may be encountered in the Mission Canyon Formation at a TVD of approximately 8736'. Safety equipment will be in operation at approximately 823 6'.
A Hydrogen Sulﬁde (H2S) Contingency Plan is being prepared and will be submitted under separate cover for review and approval. As a safety precaution, the rig sub-structure will remain open during drilling operations to allow natural convection to dissipate any accumulations of hydrogen sulﬁde gas.
