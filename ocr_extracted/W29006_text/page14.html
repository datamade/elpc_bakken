DETAILS OF WORK
XTO Energy requests a waiver of the open hole logging requirement form this well. There is a cost of ~80.000 for running open hole logs on this well, including rig time and other associated equipment rentals. We will have a mud logger on this well from the Base of Last Salt formation down to total depth. This will provide a lithology and hydrocarbon show analysis of any potentially productive zones. We believe having a mud logger on the well will provide us with a better diagnostic tool than open hole logs. In addition to the mudlog, we intend to run a GRICBL log to surface.
