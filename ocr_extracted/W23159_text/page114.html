Meter Descriptions
Oil Oil will be measured through tank gauges and run tickets or corioilis meters. The coriolis meter will be a FMC Coriolis Promass 83F meter, MicroMotion F-Series, or a Micro-Motion CMF 200.
Water Water will be measured through tank gauges and run tickets and meters. The meter will be a FMC
Coriolis Promass 83F meter, MicroMotion F-Series, Micro-Motion CMF 200, mag meter, NuFlo MC—ll+ turbine meter, or NuFlow MC—lll turbine meter.

Gas Gas will be measured via v-cone or orifice plate meter.

Well Tests Well tests will be done every 90 days
