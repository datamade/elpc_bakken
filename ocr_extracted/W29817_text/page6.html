Samples typically showed light blue gray to light gray brown dolomite with dull blue yellow fluorescence and a slow weak cut. Through most of the lateral, total gas typically averaged around 300 — 800 units with slight increases at connections. Well was drilled at an average target inclination 90.4 degrees. Average formation dip for the entire lateral was estimated to be 90.3 degrees, even though formation dips averaged 90.58 degrees for the majority of the lateral. Average formation dip was 0.03 degrees above the original anticipated dip of 90.27 degrees. Upon reaching total depth of the lateral it was estimated well was drilled 70% in zone.
