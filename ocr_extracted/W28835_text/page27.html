This will be an expansion to the existing 2-well CTB currently there. This CTB will be expanded to accommodate production from 6 total wells. Produced emulsion from each well will be independently flowlined to a dedicated heater treater. Immediately prior to oil sales through common LACTs, oil will be metered from the each treater. Gas will be sold directly from each treater through meters dedicated to each treater. Water from each well will be stored in common produced water tanks and removed from location via pipeline.
