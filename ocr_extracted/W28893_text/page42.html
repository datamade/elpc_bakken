Cementing issues/cement job summary:

HALUBUFITON Cementing Job Summary

Issues with remediating the well:
The Kemper was planned for a 36 stage completion consisting of 30 sliding sleeves and 6 plug and perf stages. The casing design is a long string and therefor has no liner top to sting a frac string into. Were some form of tie-back to be run with a packer, or if a remedial cement job were to be pumped, 3.5” casing is the largest that could be run in the 5.5”. ﬁle ID of 3.5” casing is 2.75" which is smaller than the ball size required to shift the last 12 sleeves (SLEEVE IDs: 3.544, 3.467, 3.391, 3.317, 3.244, 3.172, 3.102, 3.033, 2.9, 2.835, 2.771) and would make it very challenging to run plugs for the 6 plug and perf stages.
Additional notes:
We are aware that we need to stay in compliance with the regulations listed in 27.1 on the NDIC website. If any issues occur while completing the well, the frac crew will immediately be shut down and the State will be contacted.
