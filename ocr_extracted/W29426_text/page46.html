4. Detailed Flowback Strategy
High Rate Flowbacks: Wells will be flowed back sequentially, 2—3 wells at a time, for an estimated duration of 2—15 days per well. Anticipated gas rates will range from 3,161 —4,426 Mcfd per well.
Anticipated Date of First Production: The wells at the Kings Canyon/Teton 12 Well Facility are scheduled for first production through permanent facilities in July 2015. Exact first production date may differ due to actual time required to complete each phase of the project.
