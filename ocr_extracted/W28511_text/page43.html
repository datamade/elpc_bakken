Conclusion

The Britt 151-102-4-9—2H Lateral reached a total depth of 20,748’ MD on September 15, 2014 at 5:45 PM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20748 / INC 89.10 / AZ 178.50 / TVD 10859.82/ VS 10065.14. The Gamma ranged from 39 - 89 API with average of 69 API. Gas averaged 791 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Middle Bakken formation with poor to fair intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, light to fair brown oil staining in samples, and weak to fair milky cuts and even dull to bright yellow green ﬂuorescence. The indications of good gases, a ﬂare 1-10 feet observed while drilling, and fair oil shows in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a good oil producer. The Britt 151-102-4-9-2H will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.
Robert Kappel Wellsite Geologist
