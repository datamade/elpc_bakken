D. Other Drilling Considerations & Lessons Learned:
1. Gas Buster— Not to be used for venting off flowIine. Divert all flow to gas buster.
2. Lateral Drilling- Minimize DLS.
3. Salt Sections: Tight hole and stuck pipe may occur. Control drill +/-5' and back-ream as necessary. Spot freshwater only if severely stuck.
4. Do not rotate PDC bit inside 7" casing while displacing well with kill weight fluids. Ensure bit & motor are in open hole section.
5. Transfer OBM from storage tanks to rig tanks over the shaker screens.
