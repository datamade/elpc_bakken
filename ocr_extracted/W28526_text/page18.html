This letter is to conﬁrm that American Engineering Testing, Inc. (AET) has completed the laboratory work for the soil chemistry and physical properties of the soil for the soil cement stabilization performed at this well site. The soil has been stabilized with cement to improve the stability and load carrying capacity of the subgrade, allowing a thinner layer of aggregate
surfacing to be placed in the working and driving areas and reducing future maintenance time
and costs.

Sampling and Testing Soil samples were obtained from the site by representatives of American Engineering Testing
(AET) on June 11-13, 2014. The samples were then delivered to the Dickinson, ND ofﬁce for
speciﬁc testing.

The chemical laboratory test results were performed by American Engineering Testing. The
material was tested for sodium absorption ratio (SAR), cation exchange capacity (CEC), electric
conductance (EC) and pH.
