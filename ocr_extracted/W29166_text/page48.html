Middle Bakken

At 11,525' MD weight on bit increased as rates of penetration decreased, gamma began dropping below 150 API to conﬁrm the top of the Middle Bakken, 3’ lower than the original estimated top. This suggested a ﬁnal landing target of 1 1,306' TVD at 20 feet below the upper shale, one foot lower than ﬁnal landing target. Samples transitioned to gray argillaceous siltstone with drill rates averaging 20 - 40 ft/hr, total gas averaging 100 — 250 units. Continuing to drill ahead, wellbore dropped in structure slightly as gamma ﬂuctuated around 80-90 API. At approximately 18’ into Middle Bakken at 89.20 degrees, the curve was landed at 1 1,304” TVD, 1’ above the ﬁnal landing target. Total depth of the curve was reached at 1 1,761’ MD on December 1, 2014 at 2:45 pm CST.
