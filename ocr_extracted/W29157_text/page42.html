SURFACE DAMAGE AGREEMENT McKenzie County, North Dakota
THIS AGREEMENT is made and entered into by and between the Rolfsrud Ranch, LLLP whose address is 3601 — 113“ Avenue NW, Keene, ND 58847, hereinafter referred to as Grantor, and Newﬁeld
Production Company, whose address is 1001 - l7rh Street, Suite 2000, Denver, CO 80202, hereinafter referred to as Grantee.
WHEREAS, Grantor is the surface owner of the SEM: of Section 20, Township 152 North, Range 96 West, McKenzie County, North Dakota, (hereinafter referred to as the “‘Lands”) and
WHEREAS, Grantee desires to enter onto and cross such Lands for the purpose of constructing a drillsite pad (“Wellsite Location”) for the drilling of one or more oil and/or gas wells with such drillsite pad being located in the Sl/zSE‘A of Section 20, Township 152 North, Range 96 West, McKenzie County, North Dakota, North Dakota, (hereinafter referred to as the “Drill Site”).
NOW THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants herein contained, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufﬁciency of which is hereby acknowledged by the parties hereto, Grantor has hereby granted and conveyed to Grantee a Right—Of—Way and Easement for the purpose of drilling, completing, operating, producing and maintaining the Rolfsrud State 152-96-29-32—1H and any additional drilled on the Wellsite location, subject to the terms and conditions set forth below;
1. Grantee and its assigns or agents shall have the right to locate an access road across the subject Lands, and shall have the unrestricted right to move derricks, drilling tools, vehicles and all other machinery and equipment necessary or incident to the drilling, testing, completion and operation of oil and/or gas wells at the Wellsite Location.
2. Grantor hereby grants to Grantee a sub-surface easement and right-of—way in, under, through, and across the following described real property for the purpose of drilling wells having bottom hole locations within the drill site unit upon which the drilling pad is located or any drill site spacing unit located adjacent thereto.
3. The location of such access road is depicted on the survey plat attached hereto as Exhibit ‘A’. Grantee shall pay to Grantor the sum of One Hundred and N0/100 Dollars ($100.00) per rod for the access road. The said access road is 564 feet or 35 rods in length.
4. Grantee shall pay to Grantor the sum of Thirty Thousand and No/100 Dollars ($30,000.00) for the surface damages for the Rolfsrud State 152-96-29-32—1H well and up to two additional wells located on the Wellsite Location for the actual surface damages caused by Grantee’s operations of whatsoever nature, and this one-time payment per well to Grantor is full payment for damages sustained by Grantor and for unrestricted use of the access road and Wellsite Location.
5. Grantee agrees to pay the sum of $3,000.00 per year, as an annual rental for the access road, pad, and drillsite during the existence or use of the Rolfsrud State 152-96-29—32-1H well, and up to two additional wells drilled on the Drill Site location. The ﬁrst annual rental will become due on the next anniversary of this agreement after the completion of said well, and will terminate when the well pad is reclaimed. All monies will be paid to the Grantor, its heirs, successors or assigns in proportion to their surface ownership on the date of each annual rental payment. Grantor shall notify Grantee of any change of surface ownership and no change of ownership of Grantor’s interest shall be binding on Grantee until Grantee has been furnished with notice of said change of ownership.
6. This Agreement and the rights granted herein are effective on the date of execution by Grantor and shall continue in full force and effect so long as the Rolfsrud State 152-96-29-32-1H well or other wells located on the Lands, or land pooled therewith, are capable of producing or diligent operations are conducted.
7. No cuttings pits will be located on the Drill Site Location.
8. Cattle guards, fences, or gates will be constructed, if requested by Grantor, and gates will be kept closed at all times except when opened for passage of trafﬁc. Noxious weeds will be
