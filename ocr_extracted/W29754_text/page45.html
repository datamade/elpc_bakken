Lateral 11,278’—15,531’MD
Aﬂer casing was set at MD 11,210‘, drilling resumed at 120 pm CDT on February 12, 2015. A 10’ target zone was centered 23’ below the top of the Three Forks Bench 1. The lateral was projected on an eastbound direction (94.36 degrees) across section 36 in Township 151N/95W and offset 336" to the south. Formation dip was estimated at 89.3 across the ﬁrst third of the lateral, then ﬂattening to 89.7 degrees to TD, dropping 43‘ of TVD across the entire 4,400' lateral. Actual formation dip averaged 89.59 degrees with a ﬁnal TVD of 10,984’ at TD (MD 15,531’), with a structural drop of44’.
