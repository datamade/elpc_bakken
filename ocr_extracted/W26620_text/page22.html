Conclusion
The Petro-Hunt, LLC Glovatsky 145-98-24D-13-2H well was drilled successfully from spud to TD in 30 days (when including standby while fracing offset wells), reaching a total depth of 20,929’ MD giving this well 9,565 feet of open horizontal hole.
