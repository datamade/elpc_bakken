The top of the 10’ target window is set 11’ below the base of the lower Bakken shale. There is a consistent clean gamma contact 2’ below the top of the target window, which is used as the “set line”.
High side sliding began aﬁer drilling out because the survey shot just outside the casing was at 84.42 degrees. The Aquitard was encountered at 10,530’. After the high side sliding produced opposite results it was decided to trip out due to a possible torqued scribe line. The BHA was re-scribed and tripped back in the hole. Sliding resumed, bringing the wellbore up out of the Aquith at 11,470’. Dip calculated to 90 degrees between those points. The wellbore continued to rise in true vertical depth, reaching the gamma contact at 11,864’. Formation dip was calculated at 89.85 degrees at that point. The wellbore continued to rise in TVD, drilling in the upper 2’ of the target window until the top of the target window was exited at 12,500’.
