7. ABNORMAL CONDITIONS
No abnormal temperatures or pressures are anticipated. Bottom hole temperatures are anticipated to be 232 °F using a temperature gradient of 1.50 °F/100’.
No H28 has been encountered in or is known to exist from previous wells drilled to similar depths in the general area. H28 may be present in the Mission Canyon Formation. All H28 safety equipment will be rigged up approximately 1000’ prior to the Mission Canyon formation. Equipment will not be rigged down until the intermediate casing is set and cemented above the
H28 bearing formation.
Maximum anticipated bottom hole pressure equals approximately 7,092 psi (calculated at 0.65 psi/foot) and maximum anticipated surface pressure equals approximately 4,691 psi (anticipated bottom hole pressure minus the pressure of a partially evacuated hole calculated at 0.22 psi/foot
of hole).
