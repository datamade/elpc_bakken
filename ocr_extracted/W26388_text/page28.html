Following is the 2013 Plan of Development and the 2012 Summary of Operations for the above referenced unit:
2013 Plan of Development
It is planned to drill twenty-seven (27) unit wells during the 2013 calendar year. All of these wells will be drilled on private (fee) lands within the Unit except for one (1) which will require an approved Federal APD. One (1) of the horizontal wellbores will penetrate Federal minerals within the Unit. It is planned to convert one of the vertical monitor wells to a salt water disposal well.
