4. Detailed Flowback Strategy
High Rate Flowbacks: Wells will be flowed back sequentially, 2—3 wells at a time, for an estimated duration of 2-10 days per well. Anticipated gas rates will range from LOGO—4,000 Mcfd per well.
Anticipated Date of First Production: The CCU Golden Creek / North Coast 23 Quad Pad wells are scheduled for first production through permanent facilities in March 2015. Exact first production date may differ due to actual time required to complete each phase of the project.
