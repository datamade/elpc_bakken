AFFIDAVIT IN RE GAS CAPTURE PLAN PARSHALL 435-1113H, PARSHALL 436-1113H & PARSHALL 437-1113H WELL

STATE OF Colorado ) ) § COUNTY OF Denver )
Jennifer Yu, of lawful age, being duly sworn, on his oath says that she, Regulatory Administrator at EOG Resources, Inc., (EOG) 600 17th Street, Suite 1000N, Denver, CO 80202 and duly authorized to make this Affidavit in its behalf.
WHEREAS, EOG has applied to the NDIC for Application for Permit to Drill (APD) for the Parshall 435-1113H, Parshall 436-1113H & Parshall 437-1113H (subject wells).

WHEREAS, the North Dakota Industrial Commission (NDIC) requires operators submitting an APD to include with said application a Gas Capture Plan.
WHERAS, the NDIC requires applicant to meet with the likely gas gatherer to provide anticipated completion date and anticipated production rates of the planned well(s) to include in
said Gas Capture Plan.
NOW THEREFORE, I hereby affirm that EOG met with Pecan Pipeline Company and provided the anticipated completion date of the subject wells and anticipated production rate of
the subject wells.
