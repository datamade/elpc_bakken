Gas Plant - Hiland Partners Watford City Super System
Hiland Partners’ Watford City Super System is a network of low pressure and high pressure Eines connecting Biilings, Burke, Divide, Dunn, McKenzie, Mountrail, and Williams Counties in North Dakota in addition to Roosevelt County, Montana. Field gas is gathered and transported through the system to Hiland Partners’ Watford City Plant (Sec. 6-151N-102W) for processing. The system has interconnections
to allow offloading excess gas volumes to Hess' Tioga plant (Sec. 26-157N-95W) and to OneOK's Stateline plant (Sec. 21-155N—103W).
