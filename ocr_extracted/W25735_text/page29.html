5. Surveys
Measure-while-drilling (MWD) surveys would be recorded every 100 feet in the vertical hole, a minimum of every 30 feet while building the curve, and every 90 feet while drilling the lateral section. MWD GR would be run from the kick-off point (KOP) to lateral total depth.
