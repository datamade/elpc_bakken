Environmental Assessment and Finding of No Signiﬁcant Impact

In compliance with the regulations of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) of 1969,
as amended, an Environmental Assessment has been completed and a Finding of No Signiﬁcant Impact (FONSI) has been issued. The EA authorizes land use for two Bakken and Three Forks oil wells located atop one well pad on the Fort Berthold Indian Reservation.
All the necessary requirements of the National Environmental Policy Act have been completed.
