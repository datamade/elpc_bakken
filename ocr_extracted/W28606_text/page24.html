Metering/Allocation Procedure:

We have identified the following wells as having common ownership:

Gariety 150-99-36-25-4H well file # 27482 Gariety 150-99-36-25—5H well file # 27481 Gariety 150-99-36—25-11H well file # 28606

The gas from each well will be measured through individual orifice meters off of each well’s respective heater treater. Once gas from each well has been metered, it will be commingled in a gas scrubber upstream of the gas lift compressor. The gas will then be compressed and metered from each individual discharge orifice meter prior to being sent down hole for a gas lift application. The max injection pressure will be 1200 PSIG at a rate of 500 mcfd/well. Liquids from the gas scrubber and compressor scrubber vessels will be comingled in a tank on location and volumes determined via tank strapping. See attached facility diagram.
