Curve

The start of the curve was kicked off at 10,050’ MD. Drilling times in the curve below the kick off point were between 0.2-6.1 minutes per foot sliding/rotating. Gas varied between 281,044 units. Gamma markers, as well as lagged samples, were utilized throughout the Lodgepole, False Bakken, Upper Bakken, and Middle Bakken to correlate TVD (true vertical depth) footage values to the nearest offset wells. The curve was landed at 12:30 AM CDT on October 23, 2014 at a measured depth of 10,869 feet and a true vertical depth of 10,544.62 feet. This was 1.38 feet above the revised target prognosis of 10,546’ TVD.
