The Middle Bakken dolomite throughout the entire horizontal section was typically light bluish gray to medium bluish gray to gray, spotted very light brown, dark gray in part, microcrystalline, argillaceous, and minimally carbonaceous. The dolomite was also generally moderately ﬁrm, with fair to good intergranular porosity and light green to dark yellow to dark green ﬂuorescence, with fast streaming cuts. ROP in the horizontal ranged from 0.10 to 8.10 minutes per foot while rotating and sliding, and gas measurements ranged from 37 to 5,237 units, with max gas value within range observed at 15,634’ MD.
