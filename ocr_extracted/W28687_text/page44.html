5. This easement will remain in effect for so long as oil or gas drilling or production operations continue at the well site. The kind of production required to keep this easement in force is commercial production during at least 10
days each month. However, if the North Dakota Industrial Commission grants temporarily abandoned status to the well on the well site, that status will preserve this easement for one year.
