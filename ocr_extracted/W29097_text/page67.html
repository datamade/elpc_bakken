COMPLETION
The planned top of the 4.5” 11.6” production liner will be 50-100 ft above the kick off point.
This liner will be cemented throughout the lateral and a wet shoe sub will be set at the toe of the liner. The frac string will be 12.75# P-110 with TSHP threads and stung into a PBR run
with the liner hanger prior to the fracturing treatment. Current plans are for 28 stages using a plug and perf approach.
No diesel fuels, as deﬁned by the United States Environmental Protection Agency (USEPA), will be used in the hydraulic fracturing of this well.
