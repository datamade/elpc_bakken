Conclusion
The Curtis Moen 41—26—3H was drilled in 25.53 days from spud to TD. The original wellbore was abandoned at 19,098’ MD. A sidetrack was made at 15,891 ’ MD. The original hole up to and including the sidetrack was completed 100% within the Middle Bakken pay zone. Total depth was reached at 2:45 PM CST, February 10‘", 2015 at 20,664' MD and ll,126.6 TVD. NCS personnel logged a total of 14,571' of wellbore. The Curtis Moen is currently awaiting production operations.
