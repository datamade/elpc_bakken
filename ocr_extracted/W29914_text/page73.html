1-22-15 Curtis Moen 41-26-3H Casing Repair and Squeeze Job
Summary: Run 7" casing, work stuck pipe @ 11,293', rig up wireline 8: run free point, perforate @ 10,865', establish circulation, rig down wireline, CBU, rig up cementers & cement.
Ran 206joints of 7" HCP-110 LTC casing. Filling every 50joints. Took up and down weights at 11,200'. Up wt was 400k, down wt was 240k.
Tripping through curve slowly when the casing string started taking wt. We slacked off to 200k then attempted to pick up to see if we could get out of it before slacking off any more wt.
Picked up to 475k without being free, Casing stuck at 11,292'. Made up the circulating swedge and attempted to pump with no success.
Not able to pump any mud away, tried pumping away 200 psi to 500 psi while working pipe.
Worked pipe pulling a max of 525k and slacking off all the casing wt.
Shut well in and tried reverse circulating at 1.5 bpm at 650 psi, pumping 10 bbls away.
Opened the well back up and worked pipe with no success.
Pressured up to 3,300 psi down the inside of casing while pulling up to 475k as a last resort to try and hydraulic casing loose at the end with no success. The string held the 3,300 psi without bleeding off. We never were able to pump any mud away from the inside of the casing. Casing was stuck with casing shoe @ 11,292' MD. Lubricate draw tool, top drive, and rig.
Work stuck casing while waiting for wireline truck. 450k up weight & 50k down weight, no circulation. Hold PJSM, R/U High Plains wireline, Run free point (no movement at 10,910, 40% free @ 10,865', 100% free @ 10,736'), change wireline tools and run guns in to perforate 7" casing at 10,865', Establish Circulation, RD wireline.
CirCulate BU(3,900 stks), begin @ 1.7 bpm/135 psi, 2.5 bpm/218 psi, 3.4 bpm/w308 psi, 4.2 bpm/454 psi, 4.6 bpm w/570 psi, no losses. '
Hold PJSM, R/U, and Cement 7" casing through perforated casing @ 10,865', Fill lines with 3 bbls, and pressure test to 6,000 psi.
Begin job pumping 30 bbls tuned spacer, 80 bbls TergoVis, 68 bbls lead & 163 bbls tail cement.
