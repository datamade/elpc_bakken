Curve

Kick off point was selected within the Lodgepole at 9,860 feet measured depth, and was reached shortly after 3:00 AM CST on January 9“, 2015. A trip was performed, and the curve assembly, including a Baker TX505 bit was picked up and tripped in the hole. Drilling of the curve began shortly before 8:00 PM CST on January 9TH, 2015.
Correlations were made with the Elmer Bartleson #14-29TFX as well as the Smith #14-29XH MWD logs using seven individual Lodgepole correlative markers and formation tops. The initial landing target based on the geological prognosis was set at 10,446 feet true vertical depth. Prior to beginning the curve, based on the Lodgepole top, the landing target was dropped to 10,450 feet true vertical depth. Following the Three Forks top, the target was again dropped to 10,460 feet true vertical depth. Typical samples in the lower Lodgepole consisted of light to medium gray, brown, gray tan, cream, cream gray, medium tan to tan, light br0wn, very ﬁne to microcrystalline, slightly argillaceous limestone, with trace anhydrite, and scattered light brown oi1 staining.
