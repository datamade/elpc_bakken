LODGEPOLE 10,142’ MD 10,135 ’ T VD -8,070’ SS
The Lodgepole formation came in at 10,142’ MD and T VD of 10,1351 The Lodgepole was encountered 13’ low to the prognosis, and picked off of differential pressure and torque, and conﬁrmed with a sudden increase in gamma. Gamma increased up from 14.86 API units to 31.22 API units to mark the top of the Lodgepole. Samples in the Lodgepole were mostly light to medium gray tan to gray brown limestone with minimal ﬂuorescence and dull weak to slow milky yellow cuts.

FALSE BAKKEN 10,982 ’ MD 10,873 ’ TVD -8, 811 ’ SS
