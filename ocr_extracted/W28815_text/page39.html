SM Energy James 16X—35HA Horizontal Liner Cement

4 1/2” Liner Procedure
1. Make up float equipment and oentralizers as required on 4 1/2" liner and run in to total depth.
2. Hold safety meeting with Sanie/ crew, rig crew, and SM Enng regresentative. Review liner
setting procedure with Company reg. and tool hand. Rig in a Sanjel cementing unit, surface treating lines to drill pipe.
3. Fill lines and pressure test surface treating lines to 6000 psi. or as requested by SM representative.
4. Break Circulation with 10 bbls well bore ﬂuid. 5. Drop tool setting ball and pump to seat. 6. Pressure up on bail to set liner hanger as per tool hand. 7. Slack off on drill pipe to hang liner as per tool hand. 8. Pressure up on drill pipe to pump ball through ball seat as per tool hand. 9. Establish circulation. 10. Shut down circulation to rotate drill pipe to finish setting liner hanger. 11. Mix and pump the following: 1) 30 bbls One Part Invert Pre-flush + 6 gal. ACW-4 (Brine water as base ﬂuid) ***
2) 30 bbls Brine Water*** + 6 gal ACW-4 3) 845 sks Tail mixed at 13.5 ppg
4) Wash out games and lines. 5) 2.5 bbls water with 10 lbs. sugar added.

*** Brine water supplied by SM Energy
12. Drop plug and start displacement with 10 bbls water with 50 lbs. sugar continue displacement to floats at 5-6 bpm with fluid or as per SM Energy request. Slow displacement to 2 bpm for last 10 bbls. Bump plug as required for liner tools.
13. Hold pressure for 1 minute(s), then bleed back casing pressure to ensure float is holding.

14. Pull drill pipe out of liner hanger and reverse circulate drill pipe to circulate any excess cement from the top of the tool.
15. Rig down Sanjel.

NOTE: This recommendation is to be used as a guide. Job conditions and ﬁeld experience must dictate job procedures. Please check all calculations on location.
