DETAILS OF WORK SM Energy Company requests permission to commingle production from the Seattle 1-35H (19303), James 16-35H (28813), James 16X-35HA (28815), and James 16X-35HB (28812) at the Seattle 1-35H Central Tank Battery located on the Seattle 1-35H pad (different pad than the well pad of the James wells) location In the SWSE of Section 35, T152N, R100W. Individual flowlines will be ran from each wellhead to the CTB. Each well within the CTB will have one 6’ x 20’ 125 psi treater, two 400-bbi production oil tanks, oil coriolis meter downstream of the oil tanks before tyin into a LACT unit, a total produced gas meter and a gas sales custody transfer meter, and a water meter off the dump valve of the treater. The 7-well CTB (three additional wells to be drilled at a later date) will share two 400-be water tanks and a VRU will be installed on the CTB (allocation description is attached). Oil will be sold at the LACT and shipped via pipeline while the water will be trucked to the Ceynar 4-188WD. Total produced gas from each infill well will measured before being split off of the treaters into sales and flare lines where the gas sales will be metered by an outside gas gatherer for each well. Gas will be sold at the custody transfer meter on the sales line for each well before tying into a larger gas gathering line. The following information is attached: ‘ 1 .
