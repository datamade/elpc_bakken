DETAILS OF WORK Statoil respectfully requests approval to temporarily abandon the subject wellbore and move a smaller rig on location 60 to 120 days to repair the subject well. Sidewinder 104 cemented 1042' of heavy weight drill pipe and a liner running tool in the 7" 351! after setting the liner hanger. Drill pipe was backed off at 9318' and an attempt was made to wash over the fish but the wash pipe was twisted off. Top of wash pipe fish is at 9237'. The plan forward is to temporarily abandon the well. move off with Sidewinder 104 and move in a smaller rig to resume further recovery operations. The well was positive pressure tested to 2000 psi with no indication of pressure leak off. The plan for temporary abandonment will be as follows: Run Cast Iron Bridge Plug (ClBP) to setting depth of 9090’ on wireline, set ClBP and pressure test to ensure proper weilbore sealing. Trip out and set back pressure valve in wellhead. Nipple down BOPS and install Temporary Abandonment cap.
