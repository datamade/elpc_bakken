Lateral Sidetrack #1 14,942’ MD — 20,809’ MD
After the Whipstock was placed drilling of the sidetrack began on November 15, 2014 at 6:00am. Slides were put in to move the wellbore up in formation to the Ratty gamma marker. The ratty gamma marker was encountered at 15,188’ MD and the formation dip was recalculated to 9055 degrees inclination. The wellbore stayed between the ratty and clean gamma marker for the next 1000’ MD with formation dip being recalculated to 90.36 degrees. The wellbore went above the clean marker at about 16,200’ MD and tracked above there for about 500’ MD when it took a deﬂection off of the clean marker. Slides were put in after it was apparent the wellbore was heading for the shale and the formation had begun to drop off. The Upper Bakken Shale was scraped at 16,795’ MD and 10,870’ TVD. A TOOH was needed 16,875' MD to change out BHA on due to MWD failure November 16 2014 at 4:00 PM MST. Drilling resumed at 7: 10AM with a maximum trip gas of 4,336 units. After the scrape the slides brought the well bore back down in formation and the clean gamma marker was encountered again at 17,430’ MD and formation projection was recalculated to 89.80 degrees inclination. The formation continued at a down dip until the wellbore went below the ratty gamma marker at about 17,700’ MD when the formation projection was recalculated to 90.14 degrees inclination. The wellbore began to track under the ratty gamma marker in the stair step gamma for the next 1,200 when the ratty gamma marker was encountered again. The well bore moved below the ratty gamma marker and tracked in the stair step gamma for another 1,500’ MD for another 1000’ MD when the ratty marker was tagged at 20,200’ MD and the formation dip was recalculated to 90.24 inclination. The wellbore remained below the ratty gamma marker for the remainder of the wellbore. The Simpson 151~1025-8-1H Lateral reached a total depth of 20,809’ MD on November 19, 2014 at 12:45 PM MST.
