GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Triangle USA Petroleum Corporation Simpson 151-102-5-8-1H was the 3rd vertical and curve and the 1st Lateral of 3 wells located on Section 5 T151N R102W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. The Simpson 151-102-5-8-1H was spudded on September 3, 2014 with surface drilled and cased to be resumed at a later date. Patterson 320 spudded the Simpson 151-102-5-8-1H on September 3, 2014 and reached a total depth of 20,809’ MD on November 19, 2014. Wellsite geological services began at 9,200 on November 6, 2014 at 5:07 AM MST. The initial phase of the Simpson 151-102-5-81H is a vertical and curve landing in the Middle Bakken Formation. The single lateral well targeting the Middle Bakken Formation approximately 11’-15’ below the base of the Upper Bakken Shale was started on November 11,, 2014. Thirty-foot samples were collected throughout the vertical and curve with ﬁfty—foot samples collected in the lateral. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota.
