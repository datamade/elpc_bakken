Curve 10,990— 11,840’ MD
The curve was started at a kick off point of 10,990’ measured depth, 467’ below the Lodgepole. The bit and bottom hole assembly were changed out for a directional assembly at 10,990’ measured depth for drilling the curve. Penetration rates of the curve averaged 60 — 80 ft/hr while sliding. The BHA was pulled on December 23, 2014 due to slow ROP. The BHA #4 was changed out at 1 1,125’. The maximum trip gas was 1,547 units. The Curve was landed on December 24, 2014 at 9:30 P.M. CST, 25 feet into the Three Forks Formation at a true vertical depth of 1 1,516.56’. Casing was completed on December 26, 2014. Seven inch 35# weight casing was set to a measured depth of 1 1,820 feet and cement ﬁlled the remainder of the wellbore down to the curve landing measured depth of 11,840 feet.
