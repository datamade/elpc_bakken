Dear Mr. Holweger,
Following is our plan to develop the referenced spacing units in order to comply with NDIC Order #23453. The Uhlman 1-7H was completed 1/5/2015 on a 1280 acre spacing basis. The Uhlman Federal 2-7H1 (1280 spacing) will be batch drilled concurrently with the Uhlman Federal 3-7H and Pittsburgh 3-711 (2560 spacing) with an anticipated spud date of September, 2015 (See Attachment 1). These wells will be stimulated concurrently and the Uhlman 2-7H1 will be brought on production first to satisfy the two required wells in the 1280 spacing unit to be drilled and completed prior to completing the 2560 spacing wells.

Stimulation operations on the Pittsburgh/Uhlman 2 and 3 pad are expected to be complete by mid-January, 2016. In order to avoid drilling operations concurrent with stimulation operations, a rig is currently scheduled to move onto the Pittsburgh/Uhlman 6 and 7 pad (See Attachment 2) in April, 2016 to drill 4 additional wells within the 2560 acre spacing unit. Scheduling will be monitored and adjusted to commence these wells as soon possible after completion of stimulation operations on the ﬁrst pad.
Please contact me if you require any additional information.
