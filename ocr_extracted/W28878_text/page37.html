Three Forks 10,356’ MD 10,183’ TVD -7,891 ’ SS
The top of the Three Forks was picked at a decrease in rate of penetration and gamma, as well as a change in sample from black shale to medium gray siltstone. Rate of penetration in the Three Forks while drilling the curve ranged from 0.53 to 1.37 minutes per foot and averaged 0.85 minutes per foot. Total gas ranged from 90 to 544 units and averaged 409 units. Gamma ranged from 67 to 181 API and averaged 104 API. Initially samples were light medium gray siltstone with overall poor porosity. Samples had a good yellow green good streaming cut. Samples quickly transitioned to a gray tan sandstone with fair to poor porosity. Intermediate casing point was reached at 10,456’ measured depth on November 2, 2014 at 4:00 pm. Projection to bit put the wellbore at the target TVD of 10,190’ TVD at 89.75 degrees.
