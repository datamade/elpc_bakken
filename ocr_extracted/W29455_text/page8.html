The meter factor will represent the most recent valid meter factor based on quarterly master meter or tank proving
Measured daily well LACT volume will be the measured oil volume that is delivered to the commingled LACI', as recorded by the dedicated well coriolis meter, times the meter factor. Measurement will be done continuously. Meter data will be stored and archived. Measured monthly well LACI' volumes will represent the sum of the prior months measured daily well LACT volumes.
The measured monthly pad LACI' volume will represent the sum of the measured monthly well LACI' volumes from each well on the pad.
The custody transfer pad LACT volume will represent the actual volume sold through a LACI' as measured by the LACT metering and sampling system.
The monthly well LACI' sales volume will be calculated by multiplying the custody transfer pad LACF volume times the measured monthly well LACI' volume divided by the measured monthly pad LACT volume.

After LACI' installation, the water volume per well will be calculated as follows:
1. The meter factor will represent the most recent valid meter factor based on quarterly master meter proving.
2. Measured daily well volume will be the measured water volume that is delivered to the commingled tank battery, as recorded by the dedicated well meter, times the meter factor. Measurement will be done continuously. Meter data will be stored and archived.
3. Measured monthly well volumes will represent the sum of the prior months measured daily well volumes.
4. The measured monthly pad volume will represent the sum of the measured monthly well volumes from each well on the pad.
5. The custody transfer pad volume will represent the sum of the actual volume transferred through a LACT as measured by the LACI' metering system.
6. The monthly well expense volume will be calculated by multiplying the custody transfer pad
volume times the measured monthly well volume divided by the measured monthly pad volume.
7. Well expense will be calculated based on the monthly well expense volume.

The gas volume will be calculated as follows:

1. Measured daily well produced volume will be the measured gas volumes as recorded by an orifice plate or v-cone meter. Measurement will be done continuously.
2. Measured monthly well produced volume will represent the sum of the prior months measured daily well produced volumes.
3. The measured montth pad produced volume will be the summation of the measured montth well produced volumes from each well on the pad.
