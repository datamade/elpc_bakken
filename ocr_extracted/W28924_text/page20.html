Conclusion
Howo 5—33-4TFH is a two mile south lateral well targeting the Three Forks Formation in the Big Bend Field. The spud date for the well was September 18, 2014 and the well was completed on October 6, 2014 at a total depth of 20,035’ MD. The best oil shows and formation gas were detected in the sandier, less argillaceous cool gamma strata. The total formation drop of 5’ resulted in an average dip angle of 8997". The bottom hole coordinates for Howo 5-33-4TFH are 296.03' FSL & 1,968.14' FWL
