Conclusion

WPX Energy’s Roggenbuck 4-9HY reached the Target Depth of 21,3 75’ MD on September 24", 2014, at 2:30 PM CST. While building the curve, the landing target remained set at 10,385' TVD. The lateral section was drilled in a single run and averaged about 74.8' per hour, or around 1,797‘ of lateral drilling per day over the course of the lateral section. Well Site Geology was released just aﬁer 5 PM CST on September 24th, 2014, about 2 hours after the TD
