GAS CAPTURE PLANS REQUIRED ON ALL APD'S

Dear Operator:
This letter is to facilitate understanding by operators that all Applications for a Permit to Drill (APD) submitted to the NDiC Oil 8t Gas Division, received after May 31, 2014. must be accompanied by a Gas Capture Plan (GCP). The plan is part of the North Dakota Industrial Commission's newly-adopted policy on reducing gas flaring. This requirement is for all wells which will be drilled to the Bakken, Three Forks, Three Forks/Baltken or Sanish Pools. The GCP must accompany all APD permit renewals.
