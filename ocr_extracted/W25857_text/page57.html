The Statoil Oil and Gas’ Skarston 1-12—4H; was drilled as a single lateral horizontal oil well, targeting the Three Forks. It was located in SW, SW, Section 1, T152N, R99W, McKenzie County, North Dakota.
The Skarston 1-12-4H was spudded on September 17, 2014. Well site geological services consisted of two person mud-logging unit from 8,300 in the Otter Formation until, the end of the well. A Geospect was used for gas detection. Two sets of lagged samples in thirty, ﬁfty, and one hundred foot intervals were caught and saved to be archived.
