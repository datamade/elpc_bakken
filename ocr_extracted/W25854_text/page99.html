It was located in SWSW Section 1 T152N R99W, McKenzie County, North Dakota. The Skarston 1-12 #ZTFH was spudded on September 21, 2014. Mission Canyon:

The Mission Canyon was drilled at a TVD of 9,532ft. The formation was composed of limestone. The limestone was light to medium gray brown, to light cream in color. The limestone grain size was wackestone to packestone, and had moderate induration. Samples had minor amounts of anhydrite. The samples showed poor to no porosity, dull ﬂuorescence, and weak cloudy cuts.
