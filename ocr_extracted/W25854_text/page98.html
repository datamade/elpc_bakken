Middle Bakken: 11, 024ft MD 10,915ft TVD -8,959ft SS
The Middle Bakken was ﬁrst encountered in samples at a TVD of 10,915ft. The samples were composed of siltstone. It was medium gray to tan in color. The dolomite was micro to very ﬁne grained, and limey and sucrosic in part. The samples had spotty dull yellow ﬂuorescence, and fast cloudy cuts. Background gas while drilling in the Middle Bakken Siltstone was near 200 units.
