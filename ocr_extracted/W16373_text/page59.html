1.0 PROGRAM BRIEFING
This mud program is based on recent horizontal Bakken wells serviced by Baroid for HES / LYCO , EOG Resources and Amerada—Hess in the general area. In addition, Baroid has serviced over 150 horizontal Bakken wells in Montana/North Dakota. These wells were generally trouble free using OBM below surface casing with and without an intermediate string This program proposes the use of an oil base mud from below surface casing and through the curve to intermediate casing point of 10,000’ TMD with Saltwater/sweeps used to drill the lateral in the Bakken. Use of calcium chloride for additional density may be required, and we recommend a rotating head and gas buster
for the lateral sections.
