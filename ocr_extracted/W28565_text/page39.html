Upper Bakken Shale

The Bakken Shale was picked from gamma change. Samples were shale, black, non-calcareous, carbonaceous, pyritic, smooth, ﬁssile in part, blocky to sub-blocky, no porosity, abundant dull green ﬂuorescence, immediate bright milky yellow cut. Gas increased to a high of 366 units. The Upper Bakken shale came in 1’ low to prognosis.

Lower Bakken Shale

The Lower Bakken Shale was picked from gamma change. Samples were shale, black, non-calcareous, carbonaceous, rough, ﬁssile in part, blocky to sub-blocky, no porosity, abundant dull green ﬂuorescence, immediate bright milky yellow cut. Gas ranged from 79 to 250 units with connection gas up to 405 units. The Lower Bakken shale came in 1’ high to prognosis.
