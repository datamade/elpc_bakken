STATE OF Colorado
) ) § COUNTY OF Denver )
Jennifer Yu, of lawful age, being duly sworn, on his oath says that he. Regulatory
Administrator at EOG Resources, Inc., (EOG) 600 17th Street, Suite 1000N, Denver, CO 80202 and duly authorized to make this Affidavit in its behalf.

WHEREAS, EOG has applied to the NDIC for Application for Permit to Drill (APD) for the Wayzetta 66—2536H, Wayzetta 67—2536H, Wayzetta 68-2531H & Wayzetta 69-2531H (subject wells).
WHEREAS, the North Dakota Industrial Commission (NDIC) requires operators submitting an APD to include with said application a Gas Capture Plan.
WHERAS, the NDIC requires applicant to meet with the likely gas gatherer to provide anticipated completion date and anticipated production rates of the planned well(s) to include in said Gas Capture Plan.
NOW THEREFORE, I hereby affirm that EOG met with Pecan Pipeline Company and provided the anticipated completion date of the subject wells and anticipated production rate of the subject wells.
