4. THE PROPOSED CASING AND CEMENTING PROGRAM
Whiting Oil & Gas Corporation (Whiting) proposes to drill a vertical hole in the SWSW(275' FSL, 325' FWL) of Section 22 in Township 149North, Range 92West to an approximate TVD of 9960' (KOP) at which time directional drilling operations will be initiated on an azimuth direction of 325° with a 12°/100 build and will end the build at 10437' TVD, 11996' MD and an inclination of 90° located 1818' FSL and 500' FEL of Section 21 in Township 149North, Range 92West, at which point the 7” intermediate casing will land. The horizontal lateral azimuth will be held at 0° thru the Middle Bakken Pay with a hold of 90° inclination. The projected bottom hole target location of 251' FNL and 573' FEL of Section 16 in Township 149North, Range 92West. Total length of lateral is approximately 8469'.
