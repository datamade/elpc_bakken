Middle Bakken

The Middle Bakken formation was picked at 11,030’ MD, 10,873’ TVD picked 4 feet lower than prognosis, based on sample changes, ROP, and gamma ray. Background gas ranged from 11—3,530 units with average of 656 units. ROP averaged 1.17 minutes per foot with a range from 03—33 minutes per foot. Gamma showed a range of 46—133 API with an average of 66 API. Typical samples were: SANDSTONE: Gray brown to gray, light gray brown to light gray tan, tan, subangular to subrounded, moderately argillaceous, fair sorting, fair calcareous/dolomitic cementation, fair intergranular porosity, fair brown oil staining, even yellow green ﬂuorescence, rapid bright cloudy cut. Lateral #1

DRILLING
Drilling of the lateral began at 5:00 AM MST March 26, 2015. The plan for the lateral was to target 14’—24’ below the bottom of the base of the Upper Bakken Shale. Coming out of the shoe, the initial formation dip was projected at ~89.75 degrees and ~346.2 degrees azimuth.
From the shoe to 12,000’ MD dip was held at 90.1 degrees while dip calculations were made based off gamma markers in the Middle Bakken formation. At 12,300’ MD, the assembly crossed below the Green marker line as illustrated on the Well Tracker. From 12,500’—13,700’ MD, a formation dip of 90.25 degrees was calculated. The assembly dropped slowly in zone at 13,700’ MD. At this point the gamma heated up to 95-100 api units. The decision was made to slide up to bring the assembly closer to the middle of zone. Between 13,600’ and 14,800’ MD, a dip calculation of 90.5 degrees was calculated. This formation dip lasted until approximately 15,000’ MD, where the formation began its transformation to an 89.8 degree down dip.
From 15,000’-18,000’MD the formation transformed from an 89.8 degree to a 90.55 degree up dip. The assembly tracked well at the bottom of the drilling window during this time. From 18,000’ MD to total depth, the formation averaged a 90.3 degree up dip. The assembly reached total depth in the middle of the drilling window.
The Eckert Foundation 152-102—22-15-1H Lateral reached a total depth of 20,712’ MD on April 1, 2015 at 12:15 PM MST. Drilling rates in the Middle Bakken formation ranged from 0.02—49 minutes per foot with an average of 0.4 minutes per foot. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20712 / INC 90.5 / AZ 1.2 / TVD 10851.43 /VS 10066.4
