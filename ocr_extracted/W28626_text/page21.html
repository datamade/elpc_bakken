Rim Operating, Inc. (Rim) ﬁled with the North Dakota Industrial Commission - Oil and Gas Division (Commission) on September 29, 2014 an application for a Stripper Well Property
Determination for the above captioned well.
Information contained in the application indicates that the above mentioned well is a property pursuant to statute and rule, and Rim has elected to designate said well as a separate property for stripper well purposes. The well produced from a well depth greater than 10000 feet. During the qualifying period, May 31, 2013 through April 30, 2014, the well produced at a maximum eﬁicient rate and the average daily production from the well was 28.6 barrels of oil per day
during this period.
It is therefore determined that the above captioned well qualiﬁes as a “Stripper Well Property” pursuant to Section 57-51.1-01 of the North Dakota Century Code. This determination is
applicable only to the Bakken Pool in and under said property.
The Commission shall have continuing jurisdiction, and shall have the authority to review the matter, and to amend or rescind the determination if such action is supported by additional or newly discovered information. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact me.
