GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corp. Oddie 44-7H is a Middle Bakken well located in the SESE of Section 7 T154N R91W in Mountrail County, North Dakota. The initial phase of the Oddie 44—7H is a vertical and curve, landing thirteen feet into the Middle Bakken. It was then re—entered as a single lateral well in the Middle Bakken. The Oddie 44-7H spudded January 25, 2015 and reached a total depth of 19,010’ MD on February 23, 2015.
Wellsite geological services began at 8,200 feet in the Charles Formation on January 29, 2015 at 12:00 pm CST. Thirty foot lagged samples were collected. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at Whiting Oil and Gas Corp. in Denver, Colorado and at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota.
