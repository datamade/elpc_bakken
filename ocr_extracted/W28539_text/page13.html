OIL AND GAS LEASE SURFACE DAMAGE AGREEMENT

May 27, 2014

MIKE MURRAY
KODIAK OIL & GAS (USA) 1625 BROADWAY STE 250 DENVER CO 80202

INC

Re:

The undersigned, on behalf of the Board of University and School Lands (the Board), and Kodiak Oil and Gas, Inc, 1625 Broadway, Suite 250, Denver, Colorado 80202 (the Company), hereby agree that the surface damage payments shall be Sixty Seven Thousand One Hundred Seventy Eight and 80/100 Dollars, $67,178.80, for the oil well site and access road to be paid prior to drilling operations; pursuant to the provisions of N.D.C.C. Chapter 38-111, to be paid by the Company. The Company is authorized to enter and utilize approximately 10.59 acres of land located within the N2 of Section 16, Township 155 North, Range 99 West, Williams County, North Dakota, to construct, operate, maintain, and remove a four bore well site with one well bore approximately 860.0 feet from the north section line and 1285.0 feet from the west section line of this section called the P Dam State 155-99-4-16-21-13H3 oil well, a second well bore approximately 860.0 feet from the north section line and 1315.0 feet from the west section line Of this section called the P Dam State 155-99—4-16-21-13H oil well, a third well bore approximately 860.0 feet from the north section line and 1345.0 feet from the West section line of this section called the P Dam State 155—99-4—16-21-14H3 oil well, a fourth well bore approximately 860.0 feet from the north section line and 1375.0 feet from the West section line of this section called the P Dam State 155-99-4-16-21—14H oil well, and an access road approximately 175.65 rods in
length to the well site.
This agreement is for a spacing unit that includes the following described tracts: Sections 16 and 21, T155N, RQQW, Williams County. if a different spacing unit is part of an application filed with the North Dakota industrial Commission, Company shall notify the Board and provide the legal descriptions of the land included
in the new spacing unit. The Company agrees that it shall:
1. Require its representative, surveyor, and construction or reclamation contractor(s) to contact the ND Department of Trust Lands Surface Management Division at 1-701—328—2800 to arrange an on—site inspection, if determined to be necessary by the ND Department of Trust Lands, prior to siting and staking the well site and appurtenances thereto, such as a separate tank battery, roads, pipelines, communication lines, and electrical lines, or conducting reclamation thereof, and to receive approval by the ND Department of Trust Lands before construction or reclamation begin.
2. Notify the Board of any additional well bore(s) proposed for this well site prior to any site preparation or drilling and shall negotiate additional surface damage payments.
3. Not enlarge the Well site from the dimensions granted in this agreement without written permission from the Board.
