Conclusion

The Britt 151—102-4-9-3H Lateral reached a total depth of 20,633’ MD on September 22, 2014 at 11:25 PM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20633 /INC 89.00 / AZ 18360 / TVD 10846.27 / VS 1006633. The Gamma ranged from 46 - 85 AP] with average of 72 AP]. Gas averaged 1378 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Middle Bakken formation with poor to moderate intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, light to fair brown oil staining in samples, and weak to fair milky cuts and even dull to bright yellow green fluorescence. The indications of good gases, a ﬂare 1-10 feet observed while drilling, and fair oil shows in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a good oil producer, The Britt 151-102~4-9—3H will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.
