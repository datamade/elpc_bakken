GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Petro—Hunt Dolezal 145-97—7D-6—3H is a Middle Bakken well in a series of wells located in Dunn County, North Dakota within the Little Knife ﬁeld. The initial phase of the Dolezal 3H was a Vertical and Curve wellbore, drilling to a depth of 11,510’ MD. The Dolezal 3H spudded on June 13"“, 2014 and reached a total depth of 19,448’ MD on June 29th, 2014. Wellsite geological services began at 7,680’ MD in the Opeche Salt F ormation. Thirty foot lagged samples were collected. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the Petro-Hunt, LLC Corporation in Denver, Colorado. Neset Consulting Service provided the gas service on the Dolezal 3H. The Geospect Instruments were calibrated and tested before the logging depth of 7,680 feet, with the system ﬁmctioning correctly. Drill rates ranged from 017-157 minutes per foot while rotating and sliding. Background gas ranged from 17 to 9,272 units. Mud weights were recorded at 9.6 to 11.2 through the Vertical and Lateral.
