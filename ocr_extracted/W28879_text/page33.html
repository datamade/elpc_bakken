DETAILS OF WORK
Oasis Petroleum North America. LLC requests a variance to rule 43-02-03-31 requiring electrical, radioactive or other similar logs to be run to determine formation tops and zones of porosity. The surface location of this well will be very near Whiting Oil and Gas well Curren 11-14TFH (API #33-061-01797 NDIC # 21109) in NWNW, Section 14. T153N, R93W and the logs run on this well should be sufficient to determine formation tops in the vertical section of the well bore. As outlined in our application for permit to drill, Oasis Petroleum North America' LLC will run gamma ray logs from KOP to the total depth and cement bond log from the production casing total depth to surface. Two digital copies of all mud logs (one tit and one las) will be submitted to the NDIC.
