The meters used for measurement of each wells production on this pad are as follows: Micro Motion, Inc. 2” Coriolis Meter 3" Fisher (or equal) orifice plate gas meter Rosemount Inc. 2: Water Mag Meter
XTO will allocate liquid production at the central production facility to the various wells using factors determined from allocation metering of oil and water at each well’s individual treater. XTO will determine liquid allocation factors monthly for each well using a minimum of 72 hours continuously metered production during which time recycling of liquids will not occur. Gas will be continuously metered individually for each well through the gas gatherer’s custody transfer meters. XTO believes that such metering will result in an accurate determination of production from each well.
