Scallion 11,291 ’ MD 11,119’ TVD -8, 762’ SS

After seeing the Scallion top the TVD target window remained at a true vertical depth of 11,158’ @ 90 degrees based off the isopaching from the Scallion to the Three Forks target area in the offset Amegard and Rogness wells. After seeing the Upper Bakken the TVD landing target was lowered to a true vertical depth of 11,162’ @ 90 degrees. Once the Upper Bakken was encountered penetration rates increased ﬁom 80 ﬁ/hr to around 100 - 110 ft/hr while sliding.
