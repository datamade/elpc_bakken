Lateral Section
Drilling resumed on January 18, 2015 at 1:50 AM CDT in the Middle Bakken Formation. According to the structure map, it was believed this well would average a 90.2 degree up dip - expecting formation to come up a total of approximately 30 feet throughout the lateral section. The Middle Bakken in this part of the Bully Field was expected to be 33 feet thick. A target window of 10 to 18 feet into the Middle Bakken Formation was set by Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation. Overall, formation trended up slightly throughout the well. In total, formation came up approximately 31 feet in total vertical depth. Mudloggers used A/C and C/D facie contacts along with a clean gamma signature which was one to two feet above target window to correlate dip and determine structural position.
