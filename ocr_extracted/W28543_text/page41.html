2) Samples: The Operator is to begin collecting sample drill cuttings no lower than the: WM! - Sample cuttings shall be collected at: o 30' maximum intervals through all vertical and build sections. 0 100’ maximum intervals through any horizontal sections. - Samples must be washed, dried, placed in standard sample envelopes (3” x 4.5”), packed in the correct order into standard sample boxes (3.5” x 5.25” x 15.25”). - Samples boxes are to be carefully identiﬁed with a label that indicates the operator, well name, well ﬁle number, American Petroleum Institute (API) number, location and depth of samples; and forwarded in to the state core and sample library within 30 days of the completion of drilling operations.
