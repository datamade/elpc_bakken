BW-ARNEGARD STATE-15 1-100-3625H-6 WORKOVER PROCEDURE EHS:
> HESS CORPORATION REQUIRES THAT ALL EMPLOYEES AND CONTRACTORS WHO PERFORM WORK AT A HESS LOCATION PLACE A HIGH PRIORITY ON ENVIRONMENTALHEALTH, AND SAFETY (EHS)
PERFORMANCE.
> CONTRACTORS SHALL CONFORM TO ALL SAFETY PROCEDURES SET FORTH BY HESS AND OTHERS IN PERFORMANCE OF THE WORK. PERSONNEL WILLFULLY VIOLATING KNOWN SAFETY PROCEDURES OR POLICY ARE SUBJECT TO IMMEDIATE REMOVAL FROM HESS PROPERTY.
> A COMPLETE UNDERSTANDING OF THE SAFETY AND HEALTH REQUIREMENTS OF THE JOB IS CRITICAL TO THE OVERALL SUCCESS OF THE PROJECT. A SAFETY MEETING WILL BE HELD AT THE BEGINNING OF EACH WORK DAY, EACH SHIFT CHANGE. OR AT THE BEGINNING OF EACH MAJOR STEP
OF THE WORK PROCEDURE.
> NO TASK IS SO IMPORTANT THAT IT BE PERFORMED AT THE RISK OF HEALTH AND SAFETY. EVERY PERSON ON LOCATION HAS THE AUTHORITY, AND OBLIGATION. TO STOP WORK IF AN UNSAFE ACT
OR CONDITION IS OBSERVED.
> ALL WORK-RELATED ACCIDENTS, INJURIES, NEAR MISSES. AND UNSAFE CONDITIONS OR ACTS, REGARDLESS OF SEVERITY, SHALL BE IMMEDIATELY REPORTED TO AN ONSITE SERVICE COMPANY
SUPERVISOR AND A HESS REPRESENTATIVE.
> PERSONNEL ON LOCATION SHALL UTILIZE PROPER AND WELL MAINTAINED EQUIPMENT AND TOOLS NECESSARY FOR THE PARTICULAR JOB(S) BEING PERFORMED.
> PROPER PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE) WILL BE WORN BY ALL PERSONNEL ON LOCATION.
> NO HZS IS ANTICIPATED IN THE PERFORMANCE OF THE WORK; HOWEVER, OXYGEN DEFICIENT ENVIRONMENTS COULD OCCUR AND EXPOSURE TO HAZARDOUS ENERGY (HIGHLY PRESSURIZED EQUIPMENT) IS POSSIBLE. WORK WILL OFTEN REQUIRE PERSONNEL TO WORK MORE THAN 6' ABOVE GROUND LEVEL 80 PROPER FALL PROTECTION EQUIPMENT WILL BE REQUIRED.
> THE WORK AREA WILL BE MAINTAINED IN A CLEAN AND ORDERLY FASHION. MINOR SPILLS OR LEAKS OF FLAMMABLE/COMBUSTIBLE LIQUIDS MUST BE CLEANED UP PROMPTLY AND THE SOURCE OF THE
SPILULEAK REPAIRED.
> SMOKING IS ABSOLUTELY PROHIBITED EXCEPT IN DESIGNATED SMOKING AREAS.
