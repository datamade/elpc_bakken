Conclusion

The Simpson 151-102-5-8-2H Lateral reached a total depth of 20,700’ MD on December 14, 2014 at 7:20 AM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20,700 / INC 89.30 /AZ 179.10 / TVD 10,860.65 / VS 10,094.76. The Gamma ranged from 54 - 101 API with average of 83 API. Gas averaged 790 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Middle Bakken formation with poor to moderate intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, light to fair brown oil staining in samples, and weak to fair milky cuts and even dull to bright yellow green ﬂuorescence. The indications of good gases, a ﬂare 1—5 feet observed while drilling, and fair oil shows in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a good oil producer. The Simpson 151-102-5-8-2H will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.
