Continental Resources, Inc. (CRI) respectfully submits the following information conceming the
prevention of interference with existing wellbores throughout operations on the proposed Lancaster 7-28H, McKenzie County, North Dakota.
The Monson 1, NDIC Well File No. 7854, is a plugged and abandoned vertical well. Surface hole location of the Monson 1 is 1,820 ft. FNL and 660 ft. FEL, Sec. 33—T152N—R102W. Existing casing strings in the wellbore include 9 5/8 in. to 2,588 ft. and 5 1/2 in. to 13,850 ft. The 5 1/2 in. casing string is set through the Middle Bakken formation.
The proposed Lancaster 7—28H is a horizontal well targeting the Middle Bakken formation. The surface hole location is at 270 ft. FNL and 1,302 ft. FEL of Sec. 28-T152N—R102W. Bottom hole location of the wellbore will be 10,095 ft. S and 642 ft. E of SHL. Planned casing design includes a 9 5/8 in. string set at 2,190 ft. and a 7 in. string set at 11,338 ft. MD, 10,866 ft. TVD.
The proposed Lancaster 7-28H has a center-to-center radius of approximately 100 ft. from the Monson I wellbore. CRI will take precautions in order to not adversely affect the existing wellbores throughout drilling and completion operations.
