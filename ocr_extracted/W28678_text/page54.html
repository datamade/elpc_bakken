Continental Resources, Inc. (CR1) respectfully submits the following information concerning the prevention of a wellbore collision while drilling the Bjella 4—13H, NWNW Sec. 13, T159N, R96W, Williams County, North Dakota.
The McCoy 22-24 is a plugged and abandoned well located 1980’ FNL, 1830’ FWL Sec. 24, T159N, R96W. 5-1/2” casing was run to 11245’. The Bjella 4-1311 will be placed 150’ east of the well surface hole location. Field personnel will be made aware of these requirements and precautions will be taken while drilling these intervals to monitor the MWD tools for magnetic interference and to monitor drilling parameters to avoid a collision.
CRI feels that adequate precautions have been taken to prevent the possibility of a wellbore collision and accepts all responsibility should such a collision occur.
