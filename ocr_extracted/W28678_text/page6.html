Sidetrack #2 1 7,890’MD - 19,181 ’ MD
Sidetrack 2 began with time drilling from 17,890’ to 17,908’ MD. After successfully sidetracking, the bit came off the original wellbore and below the clean gamma. The bit dropped below the target zone and aﬁer being leveled off was brought up and entered the target zone again at approximately 18,865’ MD. The bit came up and looked to level off just below the clean gamma, remaining there until rotation showed a jump in angle at the end of the well, coming up slight at the end but not seeing a cool down in gamma before reaching a TD of 19,181’ MD at 5:35 am CT on September 25, 2014. BHL of sidetrack 2 was 235.87’ FSL & 1,993.63’ FWL of Section 24, T159N, and R96W.

This section showed an immediate decrease in gas and oil shows as it separated from the original wellbore with background gas averaging around 500 units and connection gases showing 100 units or less over background.
