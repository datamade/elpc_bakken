SURFACE HOLE AND CASING (60’ to 2,150’1

Drill 13-1/2" hole to 2,150’—or deeper-- (permitted depth = 2,150'). Surface casing should be run to TD. Minimum casing must set into the top of the Pierre Shale (estimated at 1930') plus 50’. Record deviation every 500'. Maximum rate of build is 1 deg per 100' and maximum total deviation allowed is 3 degrees. Mud rings and ﬂowline plugging may be a problem. Sweep hole every 500 ft with 20-30 bbl pill containing 20 ppb Bentonite, with 0.1 ppb SuperSweep, or 5 sx Sawdust. If gravel sloughing is a problem, raise viscosity to 80-120 sec/qt with Bentonite and sweep hole with pills.
This well will use a closed loop system for drilling ﬂuids. Work with Kayden/Solids Control company to rig up solids control system-centrifuges and de-watering systems. There will be no reserve pit—Drill cuttings will be hauled off to approved
disposal site.
