Dudley Dawson 6-2-11H

6” PRODUCTION HOLE

LATERAL SECTION (11,388’ MD to +/-20,864’ MD!
1.
Pick up lateral BHA and 4” drill pipe. TIH with 6” PDC bit, 4.75” Motor—7/8 Lobe 3.8 Stage, 0.52 rev/gal (1.5 deg FBH), and MWD, (2) NMDC, (1)joint of drill pipe and then a reamer. In Mongoose 1-8-5H—Spidel reamer was replaced with ZDS reamer so MWD could be retrieved by wireline.
Test MWD and motor before going into hole. Hold safety meeting with “Drill Pipe Pick-up Crew” and rig crews prior to picking up 4" drill pipe.
Prior to drilling out, ensure that hole is displaced with brine water. Drill out float collar and shoe joint. Drill horizontal section with (+/-) 9.5 ppg brine water. Note: Treat system with scale inhibitor while drilling out float equipment. Run a corrosion ring in the drill string once the 4” drill pipe is picked up.
Drill lateral through the Bakken to PBHL. Work with geologists and mud loggers to stay in zone.

Projected Target: Desired TD coordinates are 250’ FSL & 1247’ FEL in Sec 11 T148N-R103W. (10,095’ South and 676’ West of SHL) See the attached well plat / directional plan for further details. Keep lateral in 100’ wide alley—Between 1220’ FEL and 1320’ FEL (649’ West- 749’ West of SHL).
See Drilling Fluids recommendation for hole cleaning methods to reduce pack-offs and for Brine Lubricant to reduce torque and drag. Use closed system with centrifuges and de-watering systems. In horizontal sections, report drilling fluid loss daily—BOTH 24 hour loss and cumulative loss. Record all tight spots in lateral on morning report.

As well approaches offset while in lateral, slow ROP and monitor: magnetic interference, shock and vibration, torque and drag, changes in drilling fluid volumes, and appearance of metal shavings in drill cuttings.
