The gas volume will be calculated as follows:
1. Measured daily well volume will be the measured gas volumes as recorded by an oriﬁce plate or v—cone meter. Measurement will be done continuously. Triangle personnel will verify the
measurement daily. Measured meter data will be stored and archived. 2. Measured monthly well volumes will represent the sum ofthe prior months measured daily
volumes. 3. The measured monthly pad volume will represent the sum ofthe measured monthly well
volumes from each well. 4. The custody transfer pad volume will represent the actual volume delivered through a single
pipeline sales or flare meter. 5. The monthly well sales volume will be calculated by multiplying the custody transfer pad volume
times the measured monthly well volume divided by the monthly measured pad volume. 6. Well revenue will be calculated based on the monthly well delivered volume.
