Curve 10,687’ MD — 11,3 73’ MD
The curve was started at 11:35 PM CST on November 21, 2014 with an 8 3/4” Reed curve bit and a NOV 2.38 mud motor targeting the Middle Bakken formation with an initial target TVD of 11,145 feet and 10 feet into the Middle Bakken formation. The Eide 41-13—3H gamma markers were correlated to the Eide 41-13-HR and Bide 41-13-2H gamma data. Baker Hughes directional team started the curve with an average built rate of 11 degrees per 100 feet needed. While correlating gamma signatures between Eide 41—13-HR and Bide 41—13-2H, the landing target changed drastically; between 11,139 feet and 11,145 feet TVD throughout the seven Lodgepole formation markers. The remaining formation top markers of the Eide 41-13-3H were consistent with the Eide 4113-HR well, allowing the original target depth of 11,145 feet TVD to be the ideal ﬁnal target.
