The 7” intermediate casing will be landed in the horizontal section of the Bakken Formation before commencement of drilling the lateral.
Whiting may elect to have the liner isolated with swell packers or equivalent.


The surface casing will have centralizers on the bottom three (3) joints with a stop ring utilized midway on the shoe joint. Then every third joint to surface.
Casing string(s) will be pressure tested to 0.22 psi/foot of easing string length or 1,500 psi, whichever is greater (not to exceed 70% of the internal yield strength of the casing), aﬁer cementing and prior to drilling out from under the casing shoe.
The 7” intermediate casing string has been designed for service in a sour gas (H28) and salt environment. The intermediate casing has turbolizers on the bottom three (3) joints, then every other joint to 45 degrees. Centralizers will be used every other jt from 45 degrees to KOP, then every fourth joint to surface.
