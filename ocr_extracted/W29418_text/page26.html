CONCLUSION
The Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation’s State 31-3—4H is a single lateral horizontal oil well targeting the Middle Bakken and is located in NWNE Section 3, T150N, R104W, in McKenzie County, North Dakota. It was spudded on 10 Dec 2014. The vertical wellbore reached TD of 9,850’ MD (9,848’ TVD). The curve wellbore reached TD at 10,745' MI); 10,379.53' TVD. 7” casing was set at 10,734’ MD. Total depth of 20,160' MD; 10,375.46' TVD; 10,046.07' VS reached at 2120 hrs MST 22 Dec 2014.
