AFFIDAVIT IN RE HYDROLIC FRACTURE FLUIDS AS TO THE PROPOSED PARSHALL 88-3029H, PARSHALL 23-3029H, AND PARSHALL 26-3029H

STATE OF Colorado )
) § COUNTY OF Denver )
Jonathan Holt, of lawful age, being duly sworn, on his oath says that he the Denver Division Completions Manager at EOG Resources, Inc., (E06) 600 17th Street, Suite 1000N, Denver, Colorado 80202 and duly authorized to make this Affidavit in its behalf.
WHEREAS, EOG has applied to the NDIC for permits to drill the Parshall 88-3029H, Parshall 26-3029H, and Parshall 23-3029H wells (subject wells).
WHEREAS, the United States Environmental Protection Agency identifies diesel fuel as any compound identified with the following Chemical Abstract Service Registry Numbers (CASRN) 68334—30-5 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel), 68476-34-6 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel, No. 2), 68476-30-2 (Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2), 68476-31—3 (Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4), and. 8008-20-6 (Primary Name: Kerosene); and
WHEREAS, EOG utilizes hydraulic fracturing techniques to complete drilled oil and gas wells.
NOW THEREFORE, I hereby affirm that EOG does not utilize diesel fuel, as deﬁned, in
fluids used for underground hydraulic fracturing; no diesel fuel will be used in hydraulic fracturing of the subject wells.
