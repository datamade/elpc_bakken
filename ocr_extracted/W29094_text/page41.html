Upper Bakken Shale 10,964’ MD 10,810’ T VD -8, 701 ’ SS
The Upper Bakken Shale was encountered at 10,964 feet measured depth and 10,810 feet true vertical depth. The Upper Bakken Shale was picked by an increase in gamma values into the 400-500 API range. The Upper Bakken exhibited black slightly calcareous, earthy shale. Strong hydrocarbon shows were exhibited by instant bright milky cuts. Total gas readings of the Upper Bakken rose to 3,573 total gas units.
