Background
The wells are located on the same drilling pad but have diverse ownership, separate ﬁxed royalty rate and revenue distribution. This facility constructed so that the four (4) wells (Knopik, Hazel, Kary, and Piatt) will have their own treater with individual metering for oil and water production, which will then be comingled into a single tank battery from which the oil will be sold. Recycled oil will go to a “Recycle” treater for further treatment. The Recycle Treater will have no metering equipment. Gas production will be sold from each well separately. Comingling approval will allow for installation of only one LACT unit for those four wells.
