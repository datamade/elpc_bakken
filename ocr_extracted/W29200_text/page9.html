Conclusion

The Tarpon Federal 44-19-3RTF reached a total measured depth of 20,280’ MD on October 3, 2014, at 3:00 AM CDT. Formation dip began at an 89.7 degrees coming right out of the lateral casing shoe and would begin to up-dip at 14,000 to a 9003, increasing the target angle to a 90.23. The best porosity and oil shows were present at the base of our target window, about 6’ above the top of the Three Forks Aquatard. Olive green to green brown oil was oﬁen present on the shakers while in this zone, gas was also
elevated which was another way of indicating where we were in zone. The Tarpon Federal 44—19-3RTF was the third of six wells to be drilled on this pad.
