GEOLOGICAL SUMMARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation P Thomas 154—98-15W-33-28-2H3is a grassroots well located in the SW of the SE Section 33 T154N R98W in Williams County, North Dakota. It is a single lateral horizontal well targeting the middle Three Forks dolomite and blueish gray shale, drilled on a 2560—acre spacing unit. The well was spudded on January 6, 2015 and reached a total depth of 20,615’MD at 2:45 AM CDT on February 8, 2015. Clint Wold and Nevan Larson were onsite for Wellsite supervision, along with Scott Strahm and Stephanie Connolle representing Neset Consulting Service, Inc. for wellsite geological services.
Wellsite services began at the logging depth of 7,800' MD, at 10:15 PM CDT, on January 10, 2014. Two man geological services were used for the vertical, curve and lateral. A Total Gas detector was utilized for gas analysis. Samples were caught every thirty feet throughout the vertical section and curve. Samples were caught every ﬁﬁy feet through the lateral. All samples caught were saved to be archived at the ND Core and Sample Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota.
