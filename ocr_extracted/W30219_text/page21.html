Lodgepole

The top of the Lodgepole was encountered at 10,108’ MD, 10,091’ TVD picked 2 feet lower than prognosis, based on sample changes, ROP, and differential pressure. Drill rates in the Lodgepole above KOP of 10,509’ MD ranged from 023—407 minutes per foot with average penetration rate of 1.3 minutes per foot. Gas ranged between 17-370 units averaging 94 units. Typical samples were: LIMESTONE: Gray to light gray, dark gray, light gray tan, cryptocrystalline, dolomitic in part, argillaceous, fair to good induration, poor intergranular porosity, even dull green ﬂorescence, weak milky cut. Curve tools were picked up once kick off point of 10,509’ MD was reached at 2:25 PM MST on February 27, 2015.
