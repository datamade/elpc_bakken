GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Earl T. ll-6TFH is a grassroots well located in the NW NW of Section 6 T153N R92W in Mountrail County, North Dakota. The initial phase of the Earl T 11-6TFH is a vertical section, with a subsequent curve landing within the ﬁrst bench of the Three Forks F m. The Earl T 11-6TFH spudded October 14, 2014 and reached a total depth of 16,082’MD on October 27, 2014. Well site geological services began at 8,450 feet in the Charles Formation. Thirty and ﬁfty foot lagged samples were collected. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota

Vertical

Logging services began at 8,450 feet measured depth in The Charles Formation. Gas detection system was recalibrated and tested before logging depth with system functioning correctly. Drill rates ranged from 1.0-3.5 minutes per foot. Gas ranged from 11-250 units.
