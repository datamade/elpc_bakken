North Dakota Century Code (NDCC) Section 38-08-04 provides for the preservation of cores and samples and their shipment to the State Geologist when requested. The following is required on the abOVe referenced well:
1) All cores, core chips and samples must be submitted to the State Geologist as provided for the NDCC Section 3808-04 and North Dakota Administrative Code 43-02-03-38. l.
2) Samples shall include all cuttings from:

Base of the Last Charles Salt

Samples of cuttings shall be taken at 30' maximum intervals through all vertical, build and horizontal sections. Samples must be washed, dried, packed in sample envelopes in correct order with labels showing operator, well name, location and depth, and forwarded in standard boxes to the State Geologist within 30 days of the

ND Geological Survey Core Library Campus Road and Cornell Grand Forks, ND 58202
