During our site visits to perform our DCP testing we noticed some rutting near WPT-012, where it appears the semi-trucks were turning around on the well pad. The CBR value obtained from the DCP test results indicate this may be an area that will require future maintenance. Our
recommendations for gravel surfacing are presented below for the anticipated future truck trafﬁc.

The amount of gravel surfacing required to support the anticipated truck trafﬁc can be calculated based upon the CBR value obtained from our DCP test results. A design CBR value of 22.4 estimated for the stabilized soils at the well pad. The following gravel section recommendations were calculated for a 20-year design life using the AASHTO “Guide for the Design of Pavement Structures (1993)”. Periodic maintenance and repairs as recommended by AASHTO will be
required to achieve this design life.
