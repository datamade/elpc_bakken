Background
All six wells have common ownership with the same ﬁxed royalty rate and revenue distribution. All six wells are drilled from and have facilities on the same pad, the Buffalo. The facilities will be constructed to create a “bulk and test” site, which will have two heater treaters, one with metering (“test treater”) and one without (“bulk treater”). A treater inlet manifold will be installed to allow for ﬂow ﬁ‘om the wellhead to be routed to either treater. Comingling approval will allow for installation of one LACT unit for the entire site.
