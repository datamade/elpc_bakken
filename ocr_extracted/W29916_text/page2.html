Conclusions
1.) The Emerald Oil, Inc. Dagny Taggart 3-21-16H was a successfully drilled one-run horizontal well in the Middle Bakken Formation from a surface location in Section 21, T 147 N, R 103 W (325’ FSL & 2415’ FEL) to a total depth (TD) of 20566’ MD, 10588.25’ TVD, (-8387.25’ SS), with a bottom hole location in
Section 16, T 147 N, R 103 W (250’ FNL & 1913’ FWL) in McKenzie County, North Dakota

2.) Ultraviolet sample shows in this lateral would be rated as poor to fair with slow to moderately fast diffuse cuts with residual rings. Total gas shows would also be rated as fair to good. Overall porosity was poor to fair.
