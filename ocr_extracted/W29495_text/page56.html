Kodiak Oil & Gas (USA), Inc. respectfully requests a waiver to the requirement from Commission Order no. 22124, that all portions of the well bore not isolated by cement of any horizontal well in Zone VII of the Poe-Bakken Pool shall be located approximately down the east-west axis and no closer than 200 feet to the east or west boundary of the spacing unit. "Approximately down the north-south axis" is defined in the order as a "SOD-feet corridor" (250' on either side of the axis).


Due to surface and mechanical constraints, the projected 7" casing point is 972' FSL and 472' FWL of section 31, T. 152N., R. 99W. (222' outside of the 500' corridor).


The lateral will be drilled to a point within the 500' corridor in an expeditious manner after setting an drilling out from the 7" casing. The lateral, as planned, will reach the corridor at an approximate MD of 11,950' (after drilling out approx. 446' from 7"). The nearest perforation to the heel of the well will be beyond this point.
