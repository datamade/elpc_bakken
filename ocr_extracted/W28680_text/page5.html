18,000’ MD - 19,156’ MD

The bit entered this section 5’ below the top of the target zone and following dip in the clean gamma. At 18,060’ MD gamma heated up again. Once again this hot marker looked different than at the start of the well but also different than the previous section. As drilling went forward gamma readings were 50-55 counts along with previously unseen cool downs to 35 counts. At 18,570’ MD the bit took a mild bounce and came away from the hot marker, seeing a gamma cool down to 30 counts, slightly higher than seen in many spots. The bit came up as we rotated the last of the well, showing a scrape or going out the top slightly, verifying our position in zone as we reached a TD of 19,156’ MD at 7:50 am CT on October 16, 2014. BHL of the lateral was 236.22’ FSL & 676.32’ FWL of Section 24, T159N, and R96W.

This section showed very good gas and oil shows. Oil shows of 60-70% coverage were seen through the section and gas readings nearly identical to the previous section.
