GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corp. Oddie 44-7-2H is a Middle Bakken well located in the SESE of Section 7 T154N R91W in Mountrail County, North Dakota. The initial phase of the Oddie 44-7-2H is a vertical and curve, landing thirteen feet into the Middle Bakken. It was then re-entered as a single lateral well in the Middle Bakken. The Oddie 44-7—2H spudded January 24, 2015 and reached a total depth of 18,308’ MD on February 17, 2015.

Wellsite geological services began at 8,200 feet in the Charles Formation on Feb. 5, 2015 at 3:30 pm CST. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at Whiting Oil and Gas Corp. in Denver, Colorado and at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota.
