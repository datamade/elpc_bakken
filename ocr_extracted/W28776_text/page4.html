DETAILS OF WORK
The Ranger 21X—186 has pressure on the 9 518" x 7" casing strings. Approximately 500 psi on the casing. Two other wells on the pad also have pressure. The pressure was noticed by the drilling rig after drilling the third well on the pad. Knowledge of exactly where the influx is coming from is unknown. Consequently they brought cement back to the surface caslng on the remaining three (c, E, 8; F) and were able to isolate the inﬂux. The 9 518“ shoe for the G well is at 2,265‘ and T00 is at 5,510'. The rig capped the wells upon moving off location.
