Curve

The curve was started in the Lodgepole Formation at a measured depth of 9,91 7’ at 6:30 pm CT on August 12, 2014. An 8 3/ ” Security MMDSSM bit and 2.38 degree mud motor were used to start the curve. The landing target in the Middle Bakken formation was at 10,435’ TVD but was altered to 10,427’ TVD before the curve began by Statoil Geology due to the difference in the upper Lodgepole markers. While building the curve, gamma markers within the Lodgepole were consistently picked 12 to 15 feet of the prognosis This resulted a target change to 10423’ TVD. The curve was landed successfully at 9:30 am CT on August 13, 2014 at a depth of 10,721’ MD. Penetration rates throughout the curve averaged around 80 ﬁ/hr while sliding and 90 ﬁ/hr while rotating. Background gases averaged around 50-100 units, with increases in the Upper through Lower Bakken shale reaching just over 500 units.
