SITE LAYOUT WITH METERING




Metering Narrative: Double Pack contains two separation vessels with separate metering
for oil, gas and water prior to a oomingling configuration. Oil and water entering the doublepak is pumped to the oil and salt water tanks. From the oil tanks, the oil flows to the LACT for metering and t hen pumped into the oil pipeline. The salt water is metered at the salt water skid and then pumped into the salt water pipeline. After the double pack, the gas is oomingled and then metered at the gas metering building prior to entering the gas pipeline. Gas from the VRT is metered in the VRU and then metered at the gas metering building prior to entering the gas pipeline.
