Oil Continuous Measurement; Individual well (Allocated Oil) A) Volumes of Oil shall be metered at a dedicated single well treater using Micro Motion R200 series meter with a 1700 Series transmitter 1) Oil meters will be tested Quarterly using the Master Meter Method to
insure accuracy of the meter. 2) The Master Meter will be a AO Smith 3” double case PD meter
B) All oil Leaving the facility shall be metered by LACT 1) The LACT will be “Proved” an a monthly basis until the time the production drops below 2000bbls per month

Gas ContinuOus Measurement; Individual well A) Volumes of gas shall be metered at a dedicated single well treater using oriﬁce plate and EFM 1) Gas sales meter used in the measurement will be calibrated and maintained according to the gas gathering contracts with commercial gathering entities and\or industry standard practices. B) Well gas allocation meters will be checked and calibrated on a standard time span reﬂective of gas volume magnitude.

Water Continuous Measurement; Individual well

A) Volumes of Water shall be metered at a dedicated single well treater using Turbine style meter and Electronic Flow Measurement(EFM)
B) Produces water meters will be primarily be used for production reporting and assigning produced water cost to the pertinent interest owners.
