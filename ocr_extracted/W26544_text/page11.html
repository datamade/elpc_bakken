Current Flaring Information:
Field: RanCreek Field Flare%: 45% (July-14)
North Dakota Flare %: 29% (July-14)
Alternatives to Flaring: This location will be connected to pipeline prior to production. Flaring will be reduced, when required, by utilizing NGL recovery units, natural gas generators, and to run other equipment. Gas coolers are also utilized to cool the temperature of the initial gas production that is above the temperature of the pipeline specification, so it can be sold. Currently, Kodiak Oil and Gas has NGL recovery in place and operating on 28 Wells (7 Pads) where pipeline has been delayed due to ROW issues. In addition, Kodiak has contracted 2 drilling rigs in Q3 2014 that can run on dry natural gas that is a product of the NGL recovery effort. Additional measures to limit flaring will include scheduling wellwork and initial production testing to limit peak gas production.
