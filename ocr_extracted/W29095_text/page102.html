The top of the Lodgepole was picked at 10,084’ MD using the gamma marker and a slight sample change. Drill rates in the Lodgepole averaged 0.82 minutes per foot rotating. Samples were primarily limestone, light to medium gray brown, medium to dark gray, light grey in part, trace cream, off-white, crypto to microcrystalline, massive to blocky in habit, platy in part, moderately hard to soﬂ, trace to no visible porosity, pinpoint yellow ﬂuorescence, thin streaming cuts. Oil shows were present in sample throughout the Lodgepole but more prominent shows towards the bottom of the Lodgepole, however there was no return ﬂow over the shakers. Gas ranged from 34 to 9272 total units.
Curve & Tangent 1 0, 465 ’ — 12, 050’ MD
