Gas Plant — Hiland Partners Watford City Super System
23. Hiland Partners' Watford City Super System is a broad network of low pressure and high pressure lines. Field gas is gathered and transported through the system to Hiland Partners’ Watford City Plant (Sec. 6-151N—102W) for processing. The system has interconnections to allow offloading excess gas volumes to Hess' Tioga plant (Sec. 26-157N-95W) and to OneOK’s Stateline plant (Sec. 21-155N-103W).
