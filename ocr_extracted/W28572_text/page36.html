6. I Surveys

Measure-while-drilling (MWD) surveys will be recorded every 100 feet in the Vertical hole, a minimum of every 30’ in the build section (or when steering the well) and at least every 90’ in the lateral portion of the well bore while rotating. MWD GR will be run from the kick-off point (KOP) to lateral total depth.

7. Cuttings
Cuttings from this well will not be buried. They will be hauled to the nearest NDIC approved disposal site.
