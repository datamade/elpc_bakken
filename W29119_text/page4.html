Eide 31X-29 Central Tank Battery

Details of Work Cont’d:

XTO will allocate production measured at the central production facility to the various wells utilizing direct instrument measurement of oil, gas and water at each well’s individual 3-phase separator. Measurement of production from each well will be continuous and will be logged in the XTO SCADA system. XTO will utilize this continuously collected data from the month as the basis for allocating production at the end of the month, but at a minimum a 24hr measurement period will be used to determine allocation factors. XTO believes that such allocation will result in an accurate determination of production from each well.
