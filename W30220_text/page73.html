Prior to LACT installation, the water volume per well will be calculated as follows:
1. Measured daily well volume will be the measured water volume that is delivered to the commingled tank battery, as recorded by the dedicated well meter. Measurement will be done continuously. Meter data will be stored and archived.
2. Measured monthly well volumes will represent the sum of the prior months mea5ured daily well volumes.
3. The measured monthly pad volume will represent the sum of the measured monthly well volumes from each well on the pad.
4. The custody transfer pad volume will represent the sum of the actual volume removed from location via truck run tickets for the entire production month.
5. The monthly well expense volume will be calculated by multiplying the custody transfer pad
volume times the measured monthly well volume divided by the measured monthly pad volume.
6. Well expense will be calculated based on the monthly well expense volume.
