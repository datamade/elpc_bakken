Background
The wells are located on the same drilling pad but have diverse ownership, separate ﬁxed royalty rate and revenue distribution. The Brodahl 31-25H is an existing well. The existing Brodahl 31-25H facility will be removed and a new facility will be constructed that will commingle production from the six wells
listed above. Commingling approval will allow for installation of only one permanent LACT unit for this
site.
