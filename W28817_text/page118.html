DETAILS OF WORK
The cuttings pit for the referenced well will be closed within 30 days of the well/wells being drilled and the drilling rig moving off location.
Cuttings will be dried while drilling through the use of a cuttings dryer. The cuttings will then be mixed with peat moss and hauled to the lined cuttings pit. Once the well/wells are done drilling and the rig has moved off location the cuttings pit will be buried and covered with a minimum of 4' of native soil materials.
To expedite reclamation effort, Triangle Petroleum has employed additional contractor.
