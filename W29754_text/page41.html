Curve 10,460’ — 11,278' MD
TIH with curve BHA completed at 830pm on January 25, 2015 when drilling the curve commenced. The curve was drilled with an 8 3/ ” Reed PDC bit and 2.38 degree motor. The initial TVD landing target was set at 10,942' and remained at that depth through the entire curve to landing. Correlations were made with the Luke 13-36H and Mattie 13-36H wells located on the same pad. The curve was landed in the Three Forks Bench 1 at MD 11,278' and TVD 10,940' at 120pm on February 12, 2015.
Penetration rates throughout the curve were in the 0.50 to 1.50 min/ﬂ range. Background gas remained in the 200—1000 unit range with no signiﬁcant readings through landing. The maximum gas reading of 1319 units occurred in the upper Three Forks formation.
