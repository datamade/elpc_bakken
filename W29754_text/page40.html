The Lodgepole Formation was encountered at a measured depth of 9,982’, TVD 9,972‘, 16’ high to the prognosis. The pick of the Lodgepole Formation was made primarily based on a slight change in the coloration of the limestone sample along with a slight increase in gamma from30 to 35 API.

Penetration rates through the Lodgepole Formation were inconsistent, ranging from 0.25 min/ﬂ to 0.5 min/ﬂ. Formation samples in the Lodgepole exhibited a very consistent, very hard, light to medium gray limestone with a transition to a medium to dark gray in the lower part of the formation. KOP at MD 10,460' was reached at 345am on January 25, 2015.
Total gas readings remained in the 100—200 until reaching the KOP in the Lodgepole formation. Oil shows improved in the lower portion of the Lodgepole and exhibited fair porosity.
