GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation’s Solberg ll-2TFH is located in the NWNW of Section 2 T156N R97W in Williams County, North Dakota, drilled as a multi well pad with the Solberg 21-2H and Solberg ll-2H. The Solberg ll-2H is a horizontal well with the two mile lateral across Sections 2 and 1 l, targeting the Middle Bakken. Bottom hole location is in the SESW of Section 11 T156N R97W. The Solberg ll-ZTFH was spudded August 1, 2014 at 12:00 am and reached a total measured depth of 20,607’ on August 13, 2014 at 10:00 am, 12 days from spud. Wellsite geological services provided by Jessica Stark and Jenn Frederick of Neset Consulting Service began at 7,850’. Gamma logging began at 7,200’. A Total Gas Detection system was utilized for gas analysis. Samples were caught every thirty feet throughout the vertical and curve and every ﬁfty feet throughout the lateral, as well as intermediate samples when necessary. One set of samples was washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota.
