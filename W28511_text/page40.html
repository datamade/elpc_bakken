GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Triangle USA Petroleum Corporation Britt 151-102-4-9-2H was the 3rd vertical and curve and the 2nd lateral of 4 wells located on Section 4 T151N R102W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. The Britt 151—102-4-9-2H was spudded on July 13, 2014 with surface drilled and cased to be resumed at a later date. Patterson 320 spudded the Britt 151-102-4-9-2H on August 18, 2014 and reached a total depth of 20,748’ MD on September 15, 2014. Wellsite geological services began at 9,200 on August 20, 2014 at 5:40 PM MST. The initial phase of the Britt 151-102-4-9-2H is a vertical and curve landing in the Middle Bakken Formation on August 22, 2014. The single lateral well targeting the Middle Bakken Formation approximately 11’-15’ below the base of the Upper Bakken Shale was started on September 11, 2014. Thirty-foot samples were collected throughout the vertical and curve with ﬁfty foot samples collected in the lateral. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota
