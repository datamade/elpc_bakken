To whom it may concern:
This notice is intended to confirm that the proposed completion consisting of hydraulic fracture treatment on the above mentioned well is in compliance with EPA UIC Program Guidance #84 regarding oil and gas hydraulic fracturing activities using diesel fuels. Newfield is aware of the EPA guidance and does not intend to inject "diesel fuels" in any quantity. Newfield has confirmed with its fracturing service provider that “diesel fuels” are not included in the chemicals injected during fracturing treatment of Newfield operated wells. The above referenced “diesel fuels" are described below by Chemical Abstract Service Registry Number (CASRN):

1) 68334-30-5 Primary Name: Fuels, diesel
2) 68476—34-6 Primary Name: Fuels, diesel, No. 2 3) 68476-30-2 Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2
4) 68476-31-3 Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4
5) 8008-20-6 Primary Name: Kerosene
