Conclusion
Holst 2~33MLH is a dual lateral well targeting the Middle Bakken Member of the Bakken Formation in the Big Bend Field. The ﬁrst (NENE) lateral reached a total depth of 13,534’ MD on September 8, 2014. Aﬁer completion of the ﬁrst lateral a whipstock was placed in 7” casing at 10,537’ MD and a window was milled for a second lateral. The spud date for the well was August 24, 2014 and the well was completed on September 14, 2014 after the second (NESE) lateral reached a total depth of
14,194’ MD.
Dillon Dolezal, Wellsite Geologist
