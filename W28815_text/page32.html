Allocation Procedure
SM Energy Company will use the following procedure to allocate production between the Seattle 1-35H (19303), James 16-35H (28813), James 16X—35HA (28815), and James 16X—35HB (28812) at the Seattle 1-35H
Central Tank Battery:
a. Each well will have individual flowlines, and 125 psi 6’ x 20’ vertical treaters, meters, and tanks.
b. Oil will be sold to API standards at LACT units at the CTB facility. Daily volumes will be measured by coriolis meters placed downstream ofthe oil shipping tank for each well before being sold at a shared LACT unit. Any differences in the sum of oil measured at the coriolis meters and the LACT meter reading will be measured at the end of the month and back allocated to the appropriate well.
c. Gas from each well’s treater will be sent to sales where it will be measured by an outside gas gatherer and sold. if line pressure prevents gas sales, gas will be sent to the flare line where it will be metered for each well before being sent to the shared flare stack.
d. Water from each treater will be metered off the water dump of the treater before being sent to one of the two salt water tanks. One common load line will pull from both salt water tanks and the water will be trucked off location until a gather line can be connected to a SWD well.
