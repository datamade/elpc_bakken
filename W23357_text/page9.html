Dear Mr. Eakle: This letter is to conﬁrm that American Engineering Testing, Inc. (AET) has completed the
laboratory work for the soil chemistry and hydraulic conductivity testing for the soil cement
stabilization performed at this well site. The soil has been stabilized with cement to improve the
stability and load carrying capacity of the subgrade, allowing a thinner layer of aggregate
surfacing to be placed in the working and driving areas and reducing future maintenance time
and costs.
