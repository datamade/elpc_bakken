l. Contact: The Company shall require their contractor(s) to contact the ND Department of Trust Lands. Surface Management Division. 1707 N 91h St. Bismarck, NDI 58501. 701-328-2800. two (2) weeks prior to road construction or reclamation. or if they haVe any questions.
2. Soil Reservation: Prior to construction or maintenance of the herein authorized road. the top eight (8) inches of soil shall be reserved from all areas to be disturbed including road bed, fillslope and backstope areas, as shown above. and all other areas subject to topsoil and subsoil mixing. The reserved soil shall be stockpiled and seeded to the native perennial grass seed mixture shown in Exhibit "D" such that wind and water erosion are minimized. Following soil reservation. cut and fill operations can proceed. If the road wili be permanent. all or a portion of the reserved soil shall be respread as needed to stabiiize cut or fill areas and ditches.
3. Drainage: The road shall be constructed. maintained. and removed in a manner which provides drainage adequate to maintain the existing natural flow. Structures including but not limited to cross drains. culverts. dikes, and ditches. shall be installed where necessary to maintain the natural ﬂow and to stabilize erosion. If an erosion problem develops. the necessary actions shall be taken to correct It and any erosion damage shalt be repaired.
4. Reclamation: Any road abandonment plans shall be subject to written approval of the ND Department of Trust Lands, prior to implementation. Upon abandonment of the herein authorized road. the reserved soll shall be salvaged from the road ditches, backslopes and fill slopes; gravel or scoria shall be removed; the roadbed shall be completely obliterated and recontoured to conform to the adjacent natural terrain; and the reserved soil shall be evenly
respread on the disturbed areas.
5. Revegetation: All disturbed soll surfaces resulting from construction. maintenance, or reclamation of the herein authorized road, shall be revegelated with a mixture of native perenniai grasses listed in Exhibit "D" sufﬁcient to prevent accelerated erosion and restore. as closely as possible. the original long-term productivity. The use of any other seed mixtures shall be subject to the prior written approval of the ND Department of Trust Lands. Reclamation shall not be deemed completed until erosion is controlled. the surface is revegetated with a mixture of native
grasses, and written approval is issued by the ND Department of Trust Lands.
