The blowout preventer stack:
The pipe and blind rams will be activated on each trip, but not more than once each day. All BOP drills and tests will be recorded in the IADC Log Book (Driller’s Log). At a minimum, the BOP, Choke manifold and related equipment will be pressure tested to the approved working pressure of the BOP stack (if isolated from the surface casing by a test plug) OR to 70% of the internal yield strength of the surface casing (if NOT isolated from the surface casing by a test plug). Pressure will be maintained for at least
10 minutes, or until the requirements of the test are met, whichever is longer. Testing would be performed at a minimum:
