8. ABNORMAL CONDITIONS
No abnormal temperatures or pressures are anticipated. Hydrogen sulfide (H2S) may be
encountered in the Mission Canyon Formation at a TVD of approximately 8798'. Safety
equipment will be in operation at approximately 8298'.
A Hydrogen Sulfide (H2S) Contingency Plan is being prepared and will be submitted under
separate cover for review and approval. As a safety precaution, the rig sub-structure will
remain open during drilling operations to allow natural convection to dissipate any
accumulations of hydrogen sulfide gas. Should H2S concentrations exceed 20 ppm, a "bug
blower" fan will be activated at that position in order to dissipate the concentration of H2S
gas to acceptable levels.

pressure equals approximately 3384psi (bottom hole pressure minus the pressure of a
partially evacuated hole calculated at 0.22 psi/foot at Middle Bakken target depth). The
intermediate design casing will be run and cemented before drilling the target lateral.
