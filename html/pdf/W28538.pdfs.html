GEOLOGICAL SUMMARY
The Norgard well is a Middle Bakken well located in Section 11 T149N R100W in
horizontal well targeting the Middle Bakken in McKenzie County, North Dakota. It is a
single Bakken. The Norgard was spud on 9 August 2014 and reached a total depth of MD
21412 on 21st August 2014; 12 days from spud. 9 5/8" surface casing was set to 2260
MD. 7" casing was set at 11318' MD. The mud system was invert for the vertical and
curve portion and salt water for the horizontal, mud up was at 2260' MD. Anthony Bitz
and Devin Pays of Neset Consulting Service Inc. provided mud logging and geological
services for this well. Lagged samples were caught and saved for archive at 30', 100'
intervals. Sample quality was good in the vertical and curve. At times samples were poor
in the lateral due to lube sweeps. A set of boxed samples will be sent to the state of North
Dakota and the other to Fargo Data Solutions, Inc. The formation top for the Kibbey
Lime was picked off of ROP and MWD. The formation tops for the Charles through the
Lodgepole were picked off of ROP and MWD provided by Baker MWD personnel.
