The well plan was to drill out of casing and verify landing inclination from vertical build
then drill down 3 in the Middle Bakken reservoir to the desired target zone. At our first

and then take a slight upward turn the first mile of section, then drop slightly the last mile
of the second section. The downward turn eventually presented itself almost right at the
