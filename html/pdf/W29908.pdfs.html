RUNNING PROCEDURE FOR CEMENTED LINERS

Drill well to TD; circ bottoms up. Make wiper trip to 7" intermediate csg shoe. POH & spot kill
mud in vertical hole. POH & LD drill pipe & BHA.

PU 4-1/2" liner. PU setting tool & RIH on 4" drill pipe

Land liner on bottom; PU & set hanger. Circ bottoms up.

RU cmtg company. Pump cmt slurry per design (details listed below).

Land liner wiper plug and latch into landing collar with 500 to 1000 psi over circulating pressure

Slowly increase pressure and rupture burst disk in liner wiper plug

Pump an additional 3 to 5 bbls to achieve wet shoe, shut down and check floats
