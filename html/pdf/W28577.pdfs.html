INTRODUCTION
This report summarizes the June 6, 2014 to September 8, 2014 drilling of the Rieckhoff 21X-3E. Surface
location is 325' FNL x 1670' FWL, NWNE Sec 3 T-151N R-99W. The projected path is to the Southwest
with a proposed Azimuth of 189.42°. Offset wells used for correlation include the Rieckhoff 21X-3G.
Straight hole and build section surveys were taken utilizing MS Energy MWD. Samples were collected and
gas monitored from 9550' MD in the vertical hole. Kick off for the build section was at 10925' MD. The
Three Forks was encountered at 11465' MD, 65.2° Inc, 244.7° Azi, 11356.43' TVD. The Three Forks.
The curve was landed at a depth of 11615' MD, 11383.28' TVD. Intermediate casing was set to 11595'
MD, 11382.2' TVD, 265.47' VS, 189.51' S x 479.74' E, Legal: 514.51' FNL x 1190.26' FWL Sec 3.
The Hotwire was calibrated to Williston Basin standards of 1% Methane = 100u. The chromatograph was
calibrated to 1% test gas = 100u. Background gas prior to landing the curve averaged 47 units above the
Lower Bakken Shale and 120 units throughout the Lower Bakken Shale to the landing point. The
background gas averaged 93 units in the lateral section. While drilling the vertical and build sections,
invert "Invermul RF" mud was used with a weight of 9.7 and increased to 10.00 upon entering the Three
Forks formation. During the lateral section Sodium Chloride mud was used with the weight held steady at
9.0-9.75.
The XTO Energy team identified a thirty-five foot formation in the Three Forks 1st Bench. The primary
target lithology is represented in this well as Dolostone sandstone/Shale­tan-brown, light gray-medium
gray, dark brown, gray/blue-blue/green, sub round-round, very fine-fine texture, slightly argillaceous,
friable-slightly firm. Over the course of the lateral section, one MWD tool failure occurred at 20384' MD.

North Dakota Administrative Code Section 43-02-03-19.2 states in part that all waste material associated with

leakproof to prevent any fluids from escaping the container
covered to prevent precipitation from entering the container
placard to indicate only filters are to be placed in the container
