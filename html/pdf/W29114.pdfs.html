On September 1, 2014, Newfield Production Company's Helsingborg Federal
153-96-22-27-10H was "spudded" as a Three Forks formation double section lateral

in the Central portion of the Williston Basin. Surface location of the Helsingborg Federal
153-96-22-27-10H is 4' FNL, 870' FWL, NWNW Section 22-T153N-R96W. Helsingborg
Federal 153-96-22-27-10H was planned with the ultimate goal of oil production in the
Three Forks formation. The horizontal well-path was planned to follow structure with an
estimated 89.42° average formation dip to total depth at 20,430' MD. The well bore was
planned to be drilled to the south following an azimuth along 178.39°.

The main offset well for drilling Newfield Production Company's Helsingborg

ENGINEERING:
The Helsingborg Federal 153-96-22-27-10H was drilled with Patterson-UTI #344.
Pad height of the Helsingborg Federal 153-96-22-27-10H was 2171.3' above mean sea
level with the Kelly bushing located at 2,196.3' above mean sea level, 25' above ground

The Helsingborg Federal 153-96-22-27-10H was drilled with fresh water from
surface to 1,878' MD. A switch was then made, and oil base mud was used from 1,878'
