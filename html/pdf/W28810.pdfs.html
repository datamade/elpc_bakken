SUMMARY AND CONCLUSIONS
The Fidelity Exploration and Production Company's Dorothy 1-12H is located in Section 36,
T140N-R98W. This is to be an Upper Devonian, Upper Three Forks horizontal well.
The well was spudded by a surface rig on 09/25/14 at 1400 hrs. Surface casing was set at 2229',
49 joints 9 5/8" 36# J-55 on 09/26/14. Nabors rig B28 moved in and spudded on 10/20/14 at
1630 hrs. Geologic supervision and gas monitoring began at 7750'.
