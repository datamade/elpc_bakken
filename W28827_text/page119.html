RUNNING PROCEDURE FOR CEMENTED LINERS
0 Drill well to TD; circ bottoms up. Make wiper trip to 7” intermediate csg shoe. POH & spot kill

mud in vertical hole. POH & LD drill pipe & BHA.
PU 4-1/2” liner. PU setting tool & RIH on 4” drill pipe
Land liner on bottom; PU & set hanger. Circ bottoms up.
RU cmtg company. Pump cmt slurry per design (details listed below).
Land liner wiper plug and latch into landing collar with 500 to 1000 psi over circulating pressure Slowly increase pressure and rupture burst disk in liner wiper plug
Pump an additional 3 to 5 bbls to achieve wet shoe, shut down and check ﬂoats
Hold 500psi on floats while stinging out from TOL, at ﬁrst sign of pressure drop, stop and pump 30 bbls of clean ﬁeld brine, continue to sting out from TOL and circ cmt out 4” XT by 7” annulus. Press test liner top packer. POH & LD setting tool. PU polish mill & RIH
Tag TOL. Dress off tie back receptacle. Circ bottoms up. POH & LD 4” DP.
PU & RIH w/ 4-1/2” frac string. Circ hole clean at TOL. Space out frac string. Circulate packer ﬂuid on backside. Land tie back string. ND BOPE; NU WH.
Cementing details listed below using a 7,181’ lateral.

PLUG AND PERF COMPLETION PROCEDURE IN CEMENTED LINER

RU high pressure pump truck. Pressure test 7”x 4-1/2” annulus to 3000 psi for 30 mins. Pressure test 4-1/2” frac string to 9500 psi for 30 mins (holding 500 psi on backside)
RU pressure pumping services/ frac crew. RU wireline truck w/ mast pole.
Pump 10K composite bridge plug & perf guns (3 sets 2’ X 6 spf) to TD. Set plug & position guns. Fire perf guns & POH.
Drop ball and pump first frac stage
Repeat WL run w/ plug and perf guns. Estimate 30-40 total frac stages per cemented liner.
