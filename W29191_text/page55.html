Upper Bakken Shale

The Upper Bakken Shale Member was next strata encountered, which appeared at prognosis depth, unlike the above formations. The Upper Bakken Shale was picked based on gamma and lithology changes. The samples changed to Shale: Black, brown black, very dark gray blocky, ﬁssile, carbonaceous, ﬁrm, moderately compacted, no porosity, trace dull yellow green ﬂuorescence, instant streaming milky cut. The average rate of penetration was 0.67 min/ft and background gas averaged 168 units, with a max of 728 units.
