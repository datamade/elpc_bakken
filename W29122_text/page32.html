OneOK’s system is a network of low pressure and high pressure lines connecting gathered wells to a gas processing facility. Field gas is gathered and transported through the system to OneOK’s Garden Creek plant (Sec. 35-151N-98W) for processing. The system has interconnections to allow offloading excess gas volumes to Hess’ Tioga plant (Sec. 26-157N95W) and to Hiland Partners’ Watford City Plant (Sec. 6-151N-102W).
