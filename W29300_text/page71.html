Production Liner:
The 4-1/2" production liner is 11.6 lb/ft, Grade HCP-110 with LT&C connections. The liner is run to within 10' of total depth of the lateral and the liner hanger is set at the Kick Off Point of the lateral. The liner will be cemented from TD to the liner hanger and completed using plug & perf techniques with intervals of approximately 200' - 250'.
