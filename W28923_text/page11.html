Conclusion

Howo 8-33-4TF2H is a two mile south lateral well targeting the Second Bench of the Three Forks Formation in the Big Bend Field. The spud date for the well was October 1 l, 2014 and the well was completed on November 1 2014 at a total depth of 19,929’ MD. There did not appear to be a stratum that displayed notable oil shows or formation gas; however, subtle increases in oil cuts and formation gas were at times detected above the target center. The total formation drop of 19’ resulted in an average dip angle of 8989". The bottom hole coordinates for Howo 8-33-4TF2H are 258.21‘ FSL & 1,339.74' FWL (SESW) Section 4 TlSlN R92W.
Dillon Dolezal, Wei/site Geologist
