The Bailey 4-24H2 is a Middle Bakken well located on Section 25 T150N R97W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. Mission Canyon

The top of the Mission Canyon was picked at 9,368.05’ TVD, with 0 delta to prognosis. The pick was based off of the gamma marker and sample change, along with differential pressure. Drill rates in the Mission Canyon averaged 1.12 minutes per foot with gas up to 217 units. Typical samples were gray to dark gray limestone, wackestone, argillaceous, poorly indurated, soft to ﬁrm, no to poor intergranular porosity. Cuts and ﬂuorescence shows were sporadic and questionable due to the oil-based mud.
