THE PROPOSED CASING AND CEMENTIN G PROGRAM
Whiting Oil and Gas Corporation proposes to drill a vertical hole in the NESE(2031' FSL, 739' FEL) of Section 19 in Township 147North, Range 91West to an approximate TVD of 9808‘ (KOP) at which time directional drilling operations will be initiated and will end the build at 10315' TVD, 10638‘ MD and an inclination of 90° . The projected bottom hole target location of 551' FNL and 1400’ FEL of Section 18 in Township 147North, Range 91West.

The 7” intermediate casing will be landed in the horizontal section of the Bakken Formation before commencement of drilling the lateral.
Whiting may elect to have the liner isolated with swell packers or equivalent.


The surface casing will have centralizers on the bottom three (3) joints with a stop ring utilized midway on the shoe joint. Then every third joint for a total of 17 centralizers.
Casing string(s) will be pressure tested to 0.22 psi/foot of casing string length or 1,500 psi, whichever is greater (not to exceed 70% of the internal yield strength of the casing), after cementing and prior to drilling out from under the casing shoe.
The 7” intermediate casing string has been designed for service in a sour gas (H28) and salt environment. The intermediate casing will have turbolizers on the bottom three (3) joints, then every other joint to 45 degrees. Centralizers will be used every otherjt from 45 degrees to KOP, then every fourth joint to the top of the Dakota sands.
The 4%“ liner will be centralized with standoff bands on every otherjoint.
B. Cementing Program:
Surface Casing ~ Lead with approximately 500 sx Econocem W1 + 0.25 pps Poly E Flake cement mixed at 1 1.5 ppg (yield = 2.98 ft3/sx).
Tail with approximately 225 sx SwiftCem + 0.25 pps Poly E Flake mixed at 14.2 ppg (yield = 1.49 ft3/sx). Cement will be circulated back to surface with 40% excess. Top out if necessary.
Intermediate Casing - Lead with approximately 750 sx EconoCem W4 + 0.2% HR-5 + 0.325 pps Poly E Flake cement mixed at 12.7 ppg (yield = 2.00 ft /sx).
