Upper Bakken Shale 15,636’MD 9,354’ TVD -7,450’ SS
The Middle Bakken dolomite throughout the entire horizontal section was typically light bluish gray to medium bluish gray to gray, spotted very light beige brown, dark gray in part, microcrystalline, argillaceous, and minimally carbonaceous. The dolomite was also generally moderately ﬁrm, with fair to good intergranular porosity and light green to dark yellow to dark green fluorescence, with fast streaming cuts. ROP in the horizontal ranged from 0.10 to 10.50 minutes per foot while rotating and sliding, and gas measurements ranged from 65 to 3,125 units, with max gas value within range observed at 15,277’ MD.
Conclusion
EOG Resources Parshall 88—3029H reached a total depth of 15,650’ MD at 12:15 AM CST on November 23, 2014 in the Middle Bakken, and is now awaiting completion.
