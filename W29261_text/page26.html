Horizontal Section

After the intermediate casing shoe was set to 9,697’ MD, drilling began in the horizontal section at 12:40 AM CST on November 20, 2014. The Middle Bakken top was ofﬁcially determined at 9,678’ MD/—7,457’ SS from a decrease in gamma values as the gamma tool transitioned from the UBS into the Middle Bakken dolomite. The curve was generally leveled off within the drilling window at a ~90° angle near measured depth of 10,900’ MD, and the GR Marker was ﬁrst encountered at 9,933’ MD/-7,468’ SS. The formation dip angle from 9,700’ — 10,700’ MD averaged 90 degrees, and from 10,700’ - 14,500’ MD averaged 90.15 degrees. Then the formation ﬂattened out with a slight down clip from 14,500” — 15,150’ MD, then slightly dipped upward from 15,150 — 15,650’ MD.
