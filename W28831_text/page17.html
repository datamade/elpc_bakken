3. PRESSURE CONTROL EQUIPMENT

A. Type:

Surface Hole - Annular preventer nippled up on conductor pipe with diverter system to divert possible fresh water flows.
Intermeda' 1e Hole and Lateral — Blow out preventer equipment nippled up on casing head and includes an 11" annular preventer and an 11" double ram preventer
(Possibly a third ram) with pressure rating of 5,000 psi.
The Blow Out Preventer Equipment and Choke Manifold include: 0 One Blind Ram (above Drilling Spool) - Two Pipe Rams (One above and one below Drilling Spool) o Drilling Spool with two side outlets (3' minimum on choke side and 2" minimum on kill side. 0 3" Diameter Choke Line Two (2) 3" Choke Line Valves—one of which must be remotely controlled from rig floor Two (2) Kill Line Valves with a check valve (2" minimum) Upper Kelly Cock Valve -with handles available Safety Valves and subs to fit all drill string connections in use Fill up line above uppermost preventer
B. Testing Procedure: The annular preventer: The Annular preventer will be function tested weekly. Annular preventer will be pressure tested to 50% of rated working pressure for ten (10) minutes or until provisions of test are met, whichever is longer. Testing would be performed at a minimum: 0 When preventer initially installed 0 Whenever seal subject to test pressure is broken 0 Following related repairs 0 At 30 day intervals
