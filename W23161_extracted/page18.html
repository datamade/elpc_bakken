Conclusion
The State 154-102-25-36-10TFH Lateral reached a total depth of 20,309’ MD on February 2, 2015 at 9:20 PM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20309 /INC 87.00 / AZ 180.70 / TVD 10765455 / VS 9771.66.The Gamma ranged from 28 — 136 API with average of 76 API. Gas averaged 59 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Three Forks formation with poor to fair intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, fast to slow weak milky cuts, and with even dull yellow green ﬂuorescence. The indications of milky cuts and yellow green ﬂuorescents in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a fair oil producer The State 154-102-25-36-10TFH will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.

Robert Koppel Wellsite Geologist
