AFFIDAVIT IN RE HYDRAULIC FRACTURE FLUIDS AS TO THE PROPOSED SHELL 7-3019H, SHELL 8-3019H, AND SHELL 9-3019H WELLS

STATE OF Colorado
) )§ COUNTY OF Denver ) David Campbell, of lawful age, being duly sworn, on his oath says that he the Denver Division Completions Manager at EOG Resources, Inc., (EOG) 600 17th Street, Suite 1000N, Denver, Colorado 80202 and duly authorized to make this Affidavit in its behalf.

WHEREAS, EOG has applied to the NDIC for permits to drill the Shell 7—3019H, Shell 8-

3019H, and Shell 9-3019H WELLS (subject wells).

WHEREAS, the United States Environmental Protection Agency identifies diesel fuel as any compound identified with the following Chemical Abstract Service Registry Numbers (CASRN) 68334-30—5 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel), 68476—34-6 (Primary Name: Fuels, diesel,
No. 2), 68476-30-2 (Primary Name: Fuel oil No. 2), 68476—31-3 (Primary Name: Fuel oil, No. 4), and, 8008-20-6 (Primary Name: Kerosene); and
WHEREAS, EOG utilizes hydraulic fracturing techniques to complete drilled oil and gas wells.
NOW THEREFORE, I hereby affirm that EOG does not utilize diesel fuel, as defined, in
fluids used for underground hydraulic fracturing; no diesel fuel will be used in hydraulic fracturing of the subject wells.
