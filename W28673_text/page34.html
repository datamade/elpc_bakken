Pronghorn Commingling Procedure
LYDIA 21-14TFH CBT

Oil, gas & water are tested initially and then monthly, one well at a time, through a test treater and isolated tanks for 5 days (120 hrs). After the test is complete, WOG ships the oil and water off lease either down the pipeline, where available, or via trucks. During the test period, gas is measured through a separate test meter, which is calibrated every 6 month’s by Whiting’s production technicians.
Whiting is testing for accuracy the use of Coriolis meters for oil and magnetic meters for water. If the testing is determined to be successful, it would eliminate the need to have isolated tanks during the test
period.
