GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Triangle Petroleum Corporation Wisness 150-100-23-14-11TFH well, is located in the SE/SW quarter of Section 23 TISON R100W and ends in the NE/NW quarter of Section 14 TISON R1 00W in McKenzie County, North Dakota.
The Wisness 150-100-23-14-11TFH, a horizontal well, was drilled by H&P 255 in the Three Forks targeting the ﬁrst bench 17 to 27 feet into the Three Forks formation. The Wisness 150-100-23—14-11TFH well spudded October 12th, 2014, and reached a
total depth of 21,200’ MD on October 29, 2014.

Wellsite geological services began at 8,800 feet in the Kibbey Formation. The gas detection system used was Neset Total Gas. Thirty foot lagged samples were collected in the vertical section and curve. Fiﬁy foot samples were collected in the horizontal. All lagged samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the

The Charles formation was encountered at 8,860’ MD; 8,858’ TVD and was picked due to an increase in ROP related to the Charles Salt, mixed with anhydrite. Samples consisted of a mix of Salt, Anhydrite, and Limestone. Sample characteristics showed clear, angular, crystalline, translucent, moderate anhydrite, rusty brown to orange, off white to cream white, trace limestone, white mineral to dull yellow ﬂuorescence, and no cut.
