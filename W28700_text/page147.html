WELL CONTROL PROCEDURES In the event that a kick is taken while drilling initiate the following procedure:
1. Pick up the Kelly and raise the tool joint above floor. 2. Shut down mud pump. 3. Make sure choke is closed. 4. Open HCR.
5. Close designated BOP. 6. Record standpipe pressure (SIDPP) after it is stabilized.
7. Calculate Initial Circulating Pressure (Slow Rate Circulating Pressure plus SIDPP) 8. Complete kill sheet & calculate kill weight.
9. Call RPM for decision whether to raise mud weight or drill under balanced.
10. Order out choke and mud gas separator, if required.
