Cementing Procedure (BHCT is estimated at 80 deg F)
(BHST is estimated at 90 deg F)




1) RlH with casing and float equipment as detailed above.
2) Circulate with rig pump a minimum of one casing volume at a (minimum) rate of 5 to 6 bpm.
3) Mix and pump cement at 5 to 8 bpm.
4) Drop plug and displace with brine or invert mud, (0.0773 bbllft). Check for cement returns at the surface. Slow rate to 1 bpm when 10 bbls short of displacement. Reduce rate to 1/2 bpm for the last 5 bbls of displacement.
Displacement volume to float collar is approximately: 163bbls.
5) Land plug with 500 psig above final circulating pressure.
6) Release pressure and check for flow back.
7) Test cement blends with fresh water on location prior to job.
8) Catch both wet and dry cement samples and store samples for testing at a later date, if required.
9) If cement falls back greater than 10’ below ground level, top cement with Class G containing 2% Calcium Chloride.
