***“ Baker Hughes Recommended Practices Prior to Cementing *****
1. Circulate the Hole Clean - On ﬁnal trip to bottom, prior to coming out of the hole with drill pipe, the wellbore should be circulated until the mud returns are CLEAR OF DEBRlS, and no cuttings or other materials are circulating out of the well.
