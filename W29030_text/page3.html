Background
All the wells listed above located on the same drill pad and all wells have common ownership with the same ﬁxed royalty rate and revenue distribution. The facilities will be constructed to create a “bulk and test” site, which will have two heater treaters, one with metering (“test treater”) and one without (“bulk treater”). A treater inlet manifold will be installed to allow for ﬂow from the wellhead to be routed to either treater, and the production will be commingled downstream of the treaters. Comingling approval will allow for installation of one LACT unit for the entire site.
