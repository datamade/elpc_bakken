GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Triangle USA Petroleum Corporation Britt 151-102-4-9-1H was the 4th vertical and curve and the 1“1 lateral of 4 wells located on Section 4 T151N R102W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. The Britt 151-102—4-9-1H was spudded on July 1 1, 2014 with surface drilled and cased to be resumed at a later date. Patterson 320 spudded the Britt 151—102-4-9-1H on August 24, 2014 and reached a total depth of 20,824’ MD on September 6, 2014. Wellsite geological services began at 9,200 on August 27, 2014 at 12:34 AM MST. The initial phase of the Britt 151—102-4-9—1H is a vertical and curve landing in the Middle Bakken Formation on August 29, 2014. The single lateral well targeting the Middle Bakken Formation approximately 11’-15’ below the base of the Upper Bakken Shale was started on August 31, 2014. Thirty-foot samples were collected throughout the vertical and curve with ﬁﬁy foot samples collected in the lateral. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at the North Dakota Geological Survey Core Library in Grand Forks, North Dakota.

Logging services began at 9,200’ MD in the Charles/Madison formation on August 27, 2014 at 12:34 AM MST. Gas detection system was tested before logging depth with system functioning correctly.
