DETAILS OF WORK SM Energy Company requests administrative permission to commingle production from the Beverly 2-16HN (29648), Fay 2-16HS (29647), Riley 23-16HS (29645), and Virgil 23-16HN (29646) at the Alvin 4-15 Central Tank Battery. Produced emulsion from each well will be independently flowlined to a dedicated heater treater. Immediately prior to oil sales through common LACT, oil will be metered from each treater. Gas will be sold directly from each treater through a meter dedicated to each treater. Water from each well will be stored in common produced water tanks and removed from location via pipeline. This CTB will be constructed to accept production from the 4 previously mentioned wells (coming from offsite drilling location), 5 future wells to be drilled on this CTB location, and 3 future wells to be drilled off this CTB location.
