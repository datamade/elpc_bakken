THREE FORKS 10,583’ MD 10,446’ TVD -8,252’ SS
The Three Forks formation came in at 10,583’ MD and 10,446’ TVD, this was 7’ high to the prognosis. Gamma readings dropped down to 81.47 API. Background gas averaged 495 units, peaking at 7728 units. Samples in the Three Forks consisted mostly of pinkish tan dolomite along with bluish gray platy moderately carbonaceous shale. The curve was landed at a total depth of 10,722’ MD on Tuesday, September 30, 2014 at 7:15 PM CST. 7” intermediate casing was set to 10,702’ MD.

LATERAL

The drilling of the lateral began on Thursday October 23, 2014 at 11:00 PM CST.

The casing shoe was set at 10,702’ MD. The wellbore would remain relatively level in the formation until 3 downslides were put in at 10,982’ MD to send the wellbore dropping in the formation. This drop would continue until a high side slide was put in at 11,320’ MD to level the wellbore off in the formation.
