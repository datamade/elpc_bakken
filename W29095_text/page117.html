The evaluation program may change at the discretion of the well site geologist, with prior approval from the Authorized Ofﬁcer, North Dakota Field Ofﬁce, Bureau of Land Management.
Stimulation : No stimulation or frac treatment has been formulated for this test at this time. The drill site, as approved, will be of suﬂicient size to accommodate all completion activities.

The planned top of the 4.5” 11.6” production liner will be 50-100 ft above the kick off point.
