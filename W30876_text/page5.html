THE PROPOSED CASING AND CEMENTING PROGRAM
Whiting Oil & Gas Corporation (Whiting) proposes to drill a vertical hole in the SWSE (388' FSL, 1932' FEL) of Section 19 in Township 147 North, Range 91 West to an approximate TVD of 9712' (KOP) at which time directional drilling operations will be initiated on an azimuth direction of 310° with a 12°/100 build and will end the build at 10193' TVD, 10482' MD and an inclination of 90° located 719' FSL and 2301' FEL of Section 19 in Township 147 North, Range 91 West, at which point the 7” intermediate casing will land. The horizontal lateral azimuth will be held at 0° thru the Middle Bakken Pay with a hold of 90° inclination. The projected bottom hole target location of 557' FNL and 1967' FWL of Section 18 in Township 147 North, Range 91 West. Total length of lateral is approximately 9466'.

The surface casing will have centralizers on the bottom three (3) joints with a stop ring utilized midway on the shoe joint. Then every third joint for a total of 17 centralizers.

The 7” intermediate casing string has been designed for service in a sour gas (H28) and salt environment. The intermediate casing will have turbolizers on the bottom three (3) joints, then every other joint to 45 degrees.
