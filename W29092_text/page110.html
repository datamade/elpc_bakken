7. ABNORMAL CONDITIONS
No abnormal temperatures or pressures are anticipated. Hydrogen sulﬁde (H2S) may be encountered in the Mission Canyon Formation at a TVD of approximately 9472'. Safety equipment will be in operation at approximately 8972'.
A Hydrogen Sulﬁde (H28) Contingency Plan is being prepared and will be submitted under separate cover for review and approval. As a safety precaution, the rig sub-structure will remain open during drilling operations to allow natural convection to dissipate any accumulations of hydrogen sulﬁde gas. Should HZS concentrations exceed 20 ppm, a “bug
blower” fan will be activated at that position in order to dissipate the concentration of H28 gas to acceptable levels.
