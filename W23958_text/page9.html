Per Fish & Wildlife: The proposed Liberty LR 107-1109H is not located in any Fish & Wildlife easements.
The 4-1/2" production liner is 11.6 lb/ft, Grade HCP-llO with LT&C connections. The liner is run to within 10' of total depth of the lateral. The liner hanger is set at the Kick Off Point of the
lateral or at the base of the 70° tangent (if present). The liner will typically have 20 swell packers installed on it. The swell packers divide the lateral into 21 intervals of about 450' each.
A frac string will always be run using a plug and perf method, averaging 30 stages.
