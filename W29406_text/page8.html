Procedure/Methodology for Individual Well Measurement on Multi-well & Single Tank Batten Locations with Common Interest Wellbores.
This procedure/methodology is to be used on QEP locations with common interest as deﬁned by a federally approved Communitization Agreement (CA), an approved NDIC pooling order or a voluntary pooling agreement. Based on currently NDIC approved or applied for QEP spacing units, this could imply 2 to 16 commingled wells per surface location.
Each pad with common interest wells will be setup with a “test train” and “bulk train” of equipment consisting of a two phase separator, heater treater, allocation meters, storage tanks, divert tank, and sales meters. Each wellhead is piped into both the bulk train and the test train. Directional ﬂow is managed by a manual valve that is changed by the operator. To test the wells and determine proper individual well allocations, each well on location will be run through the test train of equipment for an allotted period of time.
