GEOLOGIC SUMMARY

The Rennerfeldt 7-30H is a Middle Bakken well located on Section 19 T155N R98W in Williams County, North Dakota. The initial phase is a vertical and curve, landing 15.54 feet within the Middle Bakken Member of the Bakken Formation. The Rennerfeldt 7-30H spudded on October 7, 2014 and reached a total depth of 21,522’ MD on December 13th, 2014. The Bottom Hole Location is 236.68' FSL & 640.23' FEL Section 31 T155N R98W, Williams County, North Dakota.

Wellsite geological services began at 9,400 feet, just above the Base of the Last Charles Salt at 3 :27 PM CST on November 15‘“, 2014. Thirty foot lagged samples were collected during the straight hole and curve while one hundred foot lagged samples were caught during the lateral. All samples were washed, catalogued, and saved for archive at
