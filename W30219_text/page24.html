Conclusion
The Eckert Foundation 152-102-22—15-10TFH Lateral reached a total depth of 20,686’ MD on March 20, 2015 at 12:20 PM MST. The bottom hole location was projected at MD 20686 / INC 91.40 / AZ 35980 / TVD 10914.58 / VS 10050.48. The Gamma ranged from 44 - 136 API with average of 92 API. Gas averaged 14 units throughout the lateral. The lateral section was drilled 100% in the Three Forks formation with poor to fair intergranular porosity seen throughout the lateral, fast to slow weak milky cuts, and with even dull to bright yellow green ﬂuorescence. The indications of milky cuts and yellow green ﬂuorescents in the lithology while drilling indicate this well will be a fair oil producer. The Eckert Foundation 152-102-22-15-10TFH will be awaiting completion to go online producing oil.
