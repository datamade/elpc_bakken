State: North Dakota
County: Mountrail
Grantor: lVIildred Roggenbuck, a widow, Neil Roggenbuck, as Attorney-in—Fact P.O. Box 744 4370 91St Avenue NW New Town, ND 58763
Grantee: Continental Resources, Inc.
PO. Box 269007 Oklahoma City, OK 73126 Effective Date: October 8 2014
WHEREAS, the Grantor named above owns an undivided mineral interest in the lands described below (the “Lands”). The subject Lands owned by Grantor are described as follows:
Mountrail County, North Dakota
Township 153 North, Range 93 West Section 25: SW4
Containing 160.00 acres, more or less
AND, WHEREAS, Grantor’s ownership of the undivided mineral interest in the Lands begins at the surface and includes all depths and formations below the surface of the Lands (the “Easement Depths”), and;
WHEREAS, Grantee desires to drill horizontal wells for the purpose of producing oil and gas from a spacing unit comprised of Sections 26, 27, 34, and 35 Township 153 North, Range 93 West (the “Horizontal Wells”). The Horizontal Wells will have surface locations within Section 25, Township 153 North, Range 93 West, and;
WHEREAS, Grantee desires to acquire from Grantor a perpetual easement for the purpose of drilling the boreholes of the Horizontal Wells through the sub-surface of the Lands.
NOW THEREFORE, in and for adequate consideration the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, Grantor hereby grants and conveys to Grantee a perpetual subsurface easement for the purpose of drilling Horizontal Wells through the sub—surface of the Lands. This grant of easement is subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. This Easement is for the convenience of Grantee to enable it to produce oil and gas from a spacing unit comprised of Sections 26, 27, 34, and 35 Township 153 North, Range 93 West, and;
2. This Easement shall not be deemed an assignment, transfer, conveyance or sale of any of Grantor’s mineral interest in the Lands, and;
This Subsurface Easement Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the beneﬁt of Grantor and Grantee and their respective heirs, successors and assigns. It is executed by Grantor
and Grantee as of the date of the acknowledgement of their signatures, but shall be effective as of the Effective Date stated above.
