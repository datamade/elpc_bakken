Site Reclamation Procedure
Prior to reclaiming the well drilling site, remove all the imported aggregate surfacing material
and haul offsite or stockpile for recycling at another site. At the time of reclaiming, test the
cement stabilized soil pH using a commercially available soil pH test kit. Add 5 lbs. of . ammonium sulfate fertilizer per 1,000 square feet of the area to be reclaimed for every 0.4 points
the pH is over 8.2. Evenly distribute the ammonium sulfate fertilizer (21-0-0, 24S) over the
reclaimed area to reduce the soil pH and promote vegetation growth.

Using a Wirtgen WR 2500 S reclaimer (or similar device), reclaim the full depth of cement stabilized soil. The material should be pulverized so that a minimum of 95% passes the 1-inch
sieve. The soil moisture content should be damp but not saturated, to reduce the possibility of
runoff of the ammonium sulfate. The moisture content of the reclaimed material should range
between 15 and 30% to have sufﬁcient moisture for the chemical reaction to take place but
reducing the probability of runoff.
